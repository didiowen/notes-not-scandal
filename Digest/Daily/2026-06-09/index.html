    Daily Journal Digest — 2026-06-09¶

    過去 24 小時新增：Transplant ID × 19、One Health × 11、Food Security × 12，共 42 篇。

    🔴 Transplant & Opportunistic Infections¶

    Nebulized amphotericin B deoxycholate in management of chronic pulmonary aspergillosis: experience from a tertiary care centre in North India¶

    Authors: Sehgal IS et al. | Journal: J Antimicrob Chemother | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1093/jac/dkag200
    TL;DR: 36例CPA使用nebulized amphotericin B deoxycholate維持治療，中位追蹤27個月；6/7-day緩解維持率84.2%，優於alternate-day的46.7%，不良事件22.2%。

    嘻嘻：吸進去也能撐住CPA，門診終於多一點底氣。

    Gene-edited live-attenuated vaccines against Toxoplasma gondii: recent advances and future frontiers¶

    Authors: Sang X et al. | Journal: Parasit Vectors | Date: 2026 Jun 07
    DOI: 10.1186/s13071-026-07454-6
    TL;DR: CRISPR-Cas9 gene-edited live-attenuated vaccines在小鼠可誘發IgG2a、IFN-γ、IL-12與T-cell反應，保護對抗T. gondii致死感染並減少tissue cysts。

    嘻嘻：CRISPR疫苗打出免疫感，T. gondii先別太囂張。

    Development and Field Validation of a Double-Antigen Sandwich Colloidal Gold Immunochromatographic Strip for Detection of Toxoplasma gondii Antibodies in Multiple Host Species¶

    Authors: Mu X et al. | Journal: Transbound Emerg Dis | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1155/tbed/5879710
    TL;DR: rSAG2-GICA可跨物種偵測T. gondii antibodies，409件動物血清陽性率2.9%–31.3%；與ELISA整體一致率雞91.3%、狗97.7%。

    嘻嘻：一條strip跨物種抓抗體，獸醫現場會很開心。

    Chronic invasive Aspergillus Fumigatus rhinosinusitis with intracranial extension¶

    Authors: Kremsmayer T et al. | Journal: IDCases | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1016/j.idcr.2026.e02618
    TL;DR: 63歲糖尿病男性慢性侵襲性Aspergillus fumigatus rhinosinusitis延伸至眼眶與顱內，逾1年amphotericin、isavuconazole、micafungin仍惡化。

    不嘻嘻：Aspergillus闖進顱內，藥單再長也很無力。

    Severe Post-Tuberculosis Bronchiectasis With Bullous Lung Disease Complicated by Acute Infective Exacerbation: A Case Report From Uganda¶

    Authors: Sandeyl AA et al. | Journal: Clin Case Rep | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1002/ccr3.72854
    TL;DR: 烏干達60歲男性suboptimally treated PTB後嚴重bronchiectasis與bullae，痰培養長Pseudomonas aeruginosa、Streptococcus pneumoniae；culture-directed therapy後改善。

    嘻嘻：舊TB爛攤子還能救，培養結果真的是方向燈。

    Trends in incidence and DALYs of infectious disease from 2000 to 2023 and the attributable risk factors in China: an analysis of the Global Burden of Disease Study 2023¶

    Authors: Zhou L et al. | Journal: Lancet Reg Health West Pac | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.lanwpc.2026.101884
    TL;DR: 中國2000–2023年infectious disease DALYs多數下降；enteric infections ASDR由365.4降至43.8/10萬，HIV/AIDS與STI則升至103.7/10萬。

    不嘻嘻：大多往下很好，但HIV/STI偏要逆風上樓。

    Access to diagnosis using liquid biopsy (ADLiB): Identifying lymphoma in a tuberculosis-endemic setting¶

    Authors: Antel K et al. | Journal: Hemasphere | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1002/hem3.70394
    TL;DR: 南非124例lymphadenopathy中，ADLiB以plasma cfDNA區分malignant與benign，sensitivity 95%、specificity 75%、accuracy 92%，AUC 0.89。

    嘻嘻：TB盛行區用cfDNA分流，少走冤枉路太重要。

    Bone Marrow Examination in the Diagnosis of Infectious Pathology: An Often Underutilized Diagnostic Tool¶

    Authors: Maharjan M et al. | Journal: Cureus | Date: 2026 May
    DOI: 10.7759/cureus.108330
    TL;DR: 6年BME診斷29例感染，leishmaniasis占65.5%，tuberculosis與histoplasmosis各10.3%；PUO合併anemia、plasmacytosis時可提升快速診斷。

    嘻嘻：Bone marrow examination老工具，關鍵時刻仍很能打。

    Epstein-Barr virus and oral health: epidemiology, pathogenesis, clinical aspects, and diagnostic insights: a narrative review¶

    Authors: Sriram S et al. | Journal: Ann Med Surg (Lond) | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1097/MS9.0000000000005034
    TL;DR: EBV口腔表現含palatal petechiae、oral hairy leukoplakia與ulcers，並可能連結periodontitis與oral cancer；診斷需PCR、in situ hybridization與serology整合。

    不嘻嘻：EBV在口腔戲份太多，牙科也被拖下水。

    Letermovir does not affect long-term polyclonal immune reconstitution after allogeneic hematopoietic stem cell transplantation with ATG-based GvHD prophylaxis¶

    Authors: Weidt C et al. | Journal: Front Immunol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fimmu.2026.1790563
    TL;DR: 276名HCMV-seropositive allo-SCT患者中，letermovir使100天clinically significant HCMV infections由68.4%降至28.3%，18個月IR相近（24% vs 27%）。

    嘻嘻：letermovir降CMV又不亂免疫，這很可以。

    Burden of infectious diseases in children during the first year after solid organ transplantation¶

    Authors: Rock NM et al. | Journal: Transpl Int | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/ti.2026.16075
    TL;DR: 瑞士285名pediatric SOT一年內53%至少一次感染、共360次，incidence 1.36/person-year；viral infections占53%，肝與肺移植感染率最高。

    不嘻嘻：小朋友SOT第一年，感染像例行公事般報到。

    Utility of Cytopathology in Diagnosing a Plethora of Fungal Infections¶

    Authors: Suneja P et al. | Journal: J Cytol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.4103/joc.joc_43_25
    TL;DR: 5年25例FNAC診斷fungal infections，以rhino-orbital cerebral mucormycosis最多（15例）；FNAC可在臨床未疑及時協助早期治療。

    嘻嘻：FNAC抓fungal infection，便宜快狠準最香。

    Characterizing isoniazid preventive therapy uptake among people living with human immunodeficiency virus in Tanzania, a resource-constrained setting with a high tuberculosis burden: A systematic review and meta-analysis¶

    Authors: Sindato EM et al. | Journal: IJID Reg | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.ijregi.2026.100908
    TL;DR: 坦尚尼亞PLHIV的isoniazid preventive therapy uptake僅23.61%（95% CI 14.00–34.83），但完成率82.40%；IPT中TB發生率2.72%。

    不嘻嘻：IPT完成率漂亮，問題是很多人根本沒上車。

    RASGRP1 Deficiency Manifesting as Severe Vasculopathy and Fatal Autoimmune Hemolytic Anemia¶

    Authors: Ashari KA et al. | Journal: EJHaem | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1002/jha2.70275
    TL;DR: RASGRP1 deficiency病例回顧15例顯示infections 100%、lymphoproliferation 87%；新病例以severe vasculopathy與fatal autoimmune hemolytic anemia呈現。

    不嘻嘻：RASGRP1 deficiency把免疫學教成恐怖片。

    Predictors of Mucormycosis in Hospitalized Patients with Postcoronavirus Disease 2019: A Role of Metabolic and Micronutrient Factors¶

    Authors: Shabbir N et al. | Journal: Ann Afr Med | Date: 2026 Jun 05
    DOI: 10.4103/aam.aam_52_26
    TL;DR: 139名post-COVID-19患者中94例mucormycosis；diabetes 87.2%、steroid exposure 85.1%與ICU較久相關，Vitamin B12、folate異常也可能重要。

    不嘻嘻：post-COVID mucormycosis提醒我們代謝債會追來。

    Updating CMV protocols in lung transplant patients: a single-center case study modeling use of generative AI for antimicrobial stewardship protocol development and economic impact analysis¶

    Authors: Enriquez KT et al. | Journal: Infect Control Hosp Epidemiol | Date: 2026 Jun 08
    DOI: 10.1017/ice.2026.10480
    TL;DR: Microsoft CopilotTM為165例肺移植cohort建CMV prophylaxis模型，protocol未hallucination但偏保守，較guideline與院內protocol每人多花4740美元。

    不嘻嘻：AI沒亂講但很會花錢，stewardship錢包先哭。

    A rare case of orbital heavy chain amyloidosis¶

    Authors: Majumdar B et al. | Journal: Orbit | Date: 2026 Jun 07
    DOI: 10.1080/01676830.2026.2681055
    TL;DR: 65歲女性腎移植與tuberculosis病史，眼眶無痛腫塊經biopsy與tandem mass spectrometry診斷heavy chain amyloidosis；無全身侵犯可觀察追蹤。

    嘻嘻：罕見眼眶腫塊能釘出診斷，病理仍是王牌。

    Herpesvirus Retinitis by Immune Status: Clinical Phenotypes and Predictors of Retinal Detachment and Severe Visual Impairment¶

    Authors: Zou Y et al. | Journal: Ophthalmol Retina | Date: 2026 Jun 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.oret.2026.06.002
    TL;DR: 120例herpesvirus retinitis追蹤5.5年，ARN較早且較常retinal detachment；ARN（HR 11.52）與較差baseline visual acuity預測RD。

    不嘻嘻：ARN一出手就瞄準retina，眼科壓力很真實。

    Impact of fluconazole non-susceptibility on 30-day mortality in Candida tropicalis invasive candidiasis: Insights from a region with low resistance prevalence¶

    Authors: Lin GL et al. | Journal: J Glob Antimicrob Resist | Date: 2026 Jun 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jgar.2026.06.001
    TL;DR: 南台灣341例Candida tropicalis invasive candidiasis 30天死亡率45.8%、FNS 8.2%；死亡主要由宿主因素與fungemia驅動，FNS非獨立因子。

    不嘻嘻：C. tropicalis死亡率高到抗藥性都不是主角。

    🟢 One Health / Zoonoses¶

    Identification and phylogenetic analysis of seven Brucella abortus strains in Zhejiang, China¶

    Authors: Yang Y et al. | Journal: Front Cell Infect Microbiol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fcimb.2026.1758965
    TL;DR: 中國浙江7株Brucella abortus皆為ST2，ANI對參考株99.99%；cgSNP顯示與俄羅斯參考株成monophyletic cluster並分三個subclades。

    不嘻嘻：Brucella系譜又牽出跨境線索，公衛不會無聊。

    Community knowledge, attitudes and practices related to cystic echinococcosis in Ethiopia: Implications for control¶

    Authors: Efa DA et al. | Journal: One Health | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.onehlt.2026.101456
    TL;DR: 衣索比亞310人KAP調查顯示逾半對cystic echinococcosis知識有限且行為高風險；教育程度與較佳知識、安全practice強相關。

    不嘻嘻：cystic echinococcosis控制，最後還是輸給日常習慣。

    Development and implementation of an animal-inclusive Community-Led Total Sanitation program for rural Mali¶

    Authors: Dione M et al. | Journal: One Health | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.onehlt.2026.101449
    TL;DR: 馬利25村pilot中，A-CLTS組10村有6村達animal-inclusive ODF，CLTS+A為4/10，standard CLTS僅1/5；納入動物糞便管理可提升成效。

    嘻嘻：把動物糞便納入 sanitation，終於正視現場生活。

    Determinants of antimicrobial resistance practices in Ethiopia: A One Health mixed-methods study¶

    Authors: Assefa GM et al. | Journal: One Health | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.onehlt.2026.101461
    TL;DR: 衣索比亞One Health mixed-methods研究納入750戶與251名animal health professionals；attitude最穩定連結較佳AMR practice（aOR 1.26與1.50）。

    嘻嘻：AMR practice先從態度改起，One Health很務實。

    Magnolol ameliorates Streptococcus suis serotype 2-induced pulmonary inflammatory injury in mice via inhibition of GSDMD¶

    Authors: Liao J et al. | Journal: Microb Pathog | Date: 2026 Jun 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.micpath.2026.108614
    TL;DR: 小鼠與macrophage實驗顯示magnolol降低Streptococcus suis serotype 2誘發肺部發炎傷害、IL-1β/IL-18與GSDMD，效果類似GSDMD-deficient mice。

    嘻嘻：magnolol壓GSDMD，老天然物也能有新戲路。

    Genetic Characterization of Swine Influenza Viruses in Thailand in 2019-2025 Reveals Novel Reassortants¶

    Authors: Chaiyawong S et al. | Journal: Transbound Emerg Dis | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1155/tbed/9516354
