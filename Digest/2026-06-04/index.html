    🔴 Transplant & Opportunistic Infections¶

    Wickerhamomyces anomalus Fungemia during Healthcare-Associated Outbreak, Pereira, Colombia, 2025¶

    Authors: Ordoñez KM et al. | Journal: Emerg Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.3201/eid3206.251980
    TL;DR: 哥倫比亞佩雷拉2025年healthcare-associated outbreak共10例Wickerhamomyces anomalus bloodstream infection，9例為兒童、8例新生兒；6株STR顯示clonal transmission，所有病人存活。

    嘻嘻：clonal outbreak還全員存活，今天先小聲加薪給感控。

    TIME TO POSITIVITY AS A DIAGNOSTIC TOOL FOR CATHETER-RELATED CANDIDEMIA: A SPECIES-SPECIFIC ANALYSIS¶

    Authors: Albanell-Fernández M et al. | Journal: Int J Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun 01
    DOI: 10.1016/j.ijid.2026.108847
    TL;DR: 407例candidemia中，TTP可預測catheter-related來源：C. albicans ≤27小時OR 5.1、C. parapsilosis ≤35小時OR 3.1，表現優於DTP。

    嘻嘻：blood culture時鐘也會抓source，微生物室又帥一次。

    Post-Transplantation Hairy Cell Leukemia with Hemophagocytic Lymphohistiocytosis in an Adult Kidney Transplant Recipient: A Case of Rare Occurrence¶

    Authors: Ghazizadeh M et al. | Journal: Iran J Kidney Dis | Date: 2025 Sep 27
    PMID: 42234896
    TL;DR: 腎臟移植成人發生late-onset EBV-positive hairy cell leukemia合併HLH，臨床可類似sepsis；HLH與PTLD同時出現極罕見，診斷與處置困難。

    不嘻嘻：transplant後連診斷都玩變裝秀，值班醫師先跪。

    Indolent Localized Orbital Aspergillosis Mimicking Breast Cancer Metastasis¶

    Authors: Wu X et al. | Journal: Ophthalmology | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1016/j.ophtha.2026.05.004

    Helminth seropositivity in allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis: Co-exposure, misdiagnosis, or cross-reactivity?¶

    Authors: Mewara A et al. | Journal: Trop Doct | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1177/00494755261456517
    TL;DR: ABPA與helminth infections有相似clinical與immunologic特徵；回溯cross-sectional資料提示，helminth-endemic地區ABPA若反應不全或表現 atypical，應評估寄生蟲暴露。

    不嘻嘻：ABPA遇到helminth，免疫反應開始玩你猜我猜。

    Proportions of M. Intracellulare and M. Avium among non-tuberculous mycobacteria isolates in China: A Systematic review and Meta-Analysis¶

    Authors: Hou Y et al. | Journal: J Clin Tuberc Other Mycobact Dis | Date: 2026 Aug
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jctube.2026.100600
    TL;DR: 中國190項研究顯示NTM isolates中M. intracellulare占39.30%、M. avium占13.18%；HIV陽性者反轉為M. avium較常見39.46%，異質性I2>95%。

    不嘻嘻：NTM版圖又亂又異質，統合分析也只能苦笑。

    Are CD4+ T-Cell Counts Associated with Pneumocystis jirovecii Detection in Hospitalized Patients with Liver Disease? A Retrospective Exploratory Pilot Analysis¶

    Authors: Katsounas A et al. | Journal: Livers | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.3390/livers6030040
    TL;DR: 455名肝病住院者院內死亡率22.6%；22名immunological risk病人中8人做P. jirovecii PCR、3人陽性，但0例死於PJ pneumonia，死亡主因為hepatic decompensation與bacterial infections。

    不嘻嘻：肝病住院者問題很多，P. jirovecii倒像配角。

    Fourth-generation chemiluminescence QuantiFERON-TB Plus assay versus T-SPOT.TB semi-automated assay: a case for the use of both assays in immunosuppressed patients¶

    Authors: Palacios-Penedo S et al. | Journal: ERJ Open Res | Date: 2026 May
    DOI: 10.1183/23120541.01257-2025
    TL;DR: 3748名immunosuppressed病人中，任一IGRA陽性13.4%、不確定結果2%上下；QFT-Plus CLIA與T-SPOT.TB整體一致率93.0%，但HIV等族群discordance較高。

    嘻嘻：IGRA雙軌並行，immunosuppressed族群少一點盲猜。

    Comparative proteome analyses of human natural killer cells from patients after allogeneic stem cell transplantation and healthy individuals challenged with Aspergillus fumigatus¶

    Authors: Springer J et al. | Journal: Microlife | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1093/femsml/uqag020
    TL;DR: alloSCT後NK cells面對Aspergillus fumigatus刺激，day 90有1652個proteins差異、day 180降至77個；STAT1-IL27RA與GZMA、IFNg、NCAM1軸可能參與反應。

    嘻嘻：NK cells術後慢慢回神，proteome把過程拍給你看。

    Community-based health screening and all-cause mortality in rural South Africa: a longitudinal cohort study¶

    Authors: Magut F et al. | Journal: BMJ Public Health | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1136/bmjph-2025-003803
    TL;DR: 南非夸祖魯-納塔爾農村18041人參與Vukuzazi health fair，追蹤中位4.0年；參與者all-cause mortality hazard降25%（HR 0.75），5年絕對死亡率降1.5%。

    嘻嘻：community screening有救命感，公共衛生今天很有面子。

    Lead Optimization of TgCDPK1 Inhibitors for the Treatment of Toxoplasmosis¶

    Authors: Mannino MP et al. | Journal: J Med Chem | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1021/acs.jmedchem.6c00065
    TL;DR: 以X-ray structure-based design開發Toxoplasma gondii TgCDPK1 inhibitors，多個化合物具良好oral bioavailability、brain exposure與24小時PK；16c在急性感染小鼠最具preclinical潛力。

    嘻嘻：toxoplasmosis新藥苗頭不錯，腦部曝露聽起來很香。

    Common and Rare Emerging and Re-Emerging Infectious Diseases in Pediatric Transplant Recipients: A Comprehensive Review¶

    Authors: Verma A | Journal: Pediatr Transplant | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1111/petr.70365
    TL;DR: pediatric transplant recipients除CMV、EBV與bacterial bloodstream infections外，SARS-CoV-2、MDR bacteria/fungi、measles、pertussis與arboviruses等emerging pathogens負擔上升。

    不嘻嘻：pediatric transplant名單越看越長，疫苗牆也要補滿。

    Virologic and Immunological Failure and Associated Factors Among Adults Living With HIV on HAART at Jimma Medical Center, Southwest Ethiopia¶

    Authors: Teshome RT et al. | Journal: J Int Assoc Provid AIDS Care | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1177/23259582261453642
    TL;DR: 衣索比亞272名HIV成人接受HAART中，immunologic failure 8.1%、virologic failure 10.3%；poor adherence與tuberculosis co-infection或baseline viral load ≥1000 copies/mL相關。

    不嘻嘻：HIV治療失敗多半不新奇，但每次都很扎心。

    Real-world experience with gilteritinib maintenance following allogeneic transplantation in relapsed/refractory AML patients harboring FLT3 mutations¶

    Authors: Lin YT et al. | Journal: Blood Res | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1007/s44313-026-00144-3
    TL;DR: 亞洲FLT3-mutated R/R AML cohort中，gilteritinib術前salvage後接allo-HSCT與maintenance，CR率76.2%；追蹤46.2個月，maintenance與較低1年relapse及較佳生存相關。

    嘻嘻：gilteritinib接棒maintenance，AML賽後防守有亮點。

    Long-term spectrum and outcomes of post-transplant malignancy following liver transplantation: A comprehensive single-center study¶

    Authors: Megaly MG et al. | Journal: Surg Oncol | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1016/j.suronc.2026.102457
    TL;DR: 1841名成人肝臟移植者追蹤中位10.6年，25年non-HCC solid organ malignancy 18%、skin malignancy 29%、PTLD 4%；malignancy占死因20%，僅次於infection 23%。

    不嘻嘻：liver transplant活久了，malignancy也來收長期帳。

    Azole-resistant Aspergillus fumigatus and related species in Japan¶

    Authors: Tashiro M et al. | Journal: Respir Investig | Date: 2026 Jun 02
    DOI: 10.1016/j.resinv.2026.101458
    TL;DR: 日本azole-resistant Aspergillus fumigatus呈異質分布，區域監測55株中7株resistant；CPA cohort isolate-based resistance 8.3%，azole-treatment failure subgroup達46.2%。

    不嘻嘻：azole-resistant Aspergillus不均勻冒出，治療又要抖。

    🟢 One Health / Zoonoses¶

    Yellow Fever Virus Surveillance in Callithrix spp. Marmosets during Epizootic Outbreak, Brazil, 2024-2025¶

    Authors: Siconelli MJL et al. | Journal: Emerg Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.3201/eid3206.251388
    TL;DR: 巴西聖保羅州2024–2025年epizootic outbreak期間，9隻Callithrix penicillata marmosets檢出YFV，高viral load且有典型器官病灶，支持納入multispecies surveillance。

    不嘻嘻：YFV監測連marmosets都上工，人類真的別裝沒事。

    Assessing Evidence to Guide Primary Prevention of Pathogen X¶

    Authors: Holmes I et al. | Journal: Emerg Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.3201/eid3206.260293
    TL;DR: Pathogen X primary prevention可借鏡常見但人傳人差的zoonotic viruses spillover證據；在pandemic events稀少下，precautionary approach是較可行的empirical foundation。

    嘻嘻：Pathogen X還沒登場，prevention劇本先寫起來。

    In Vitro Antifungal Drug Susceptibility of Feline Sporothrix schenckii Complex Isolates, Thailand, 2023-2025¶

    Authors: Yurayart C et al. | Journal: Emerg Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.3201/eid3206.260135
    TL;DR: 泰國2023–2025年1178株貓Sporothrix schenckii complex isolates中，687株對itraconazole reduced susceptibility，含321株single-drug、270株co-reduced、96株multidrug-reduced。

    不嘻嘻：貓源Sporothrix對itraconazole冷感，毛孩也讓ID焦慮。

    Cerebrospinal Fluid Findings among Patients with Anaplasmosis and Central Nervous Involvement, Minnesota and Wisconsin, USA¶

    Authors: Dumic I et al. | Journal: Emerg Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.3201/eid3206.260240
    TL;DR: 美國明尼蘇達與威斯康辛10名anaplasmosis合併CNS症狀住院者，5人CSF正常、4人mild lymphocytic pleocytosis；CSF異常與嚴重度不相關，皆對doxycycline快速改善。

    嘻嘻：CNS anaplasmosis看似嚇人，doxycycline還是很罩。

    Regional heterogeneity of spatial-temporal characteristics and meteorological-environmental driving factors of hemorrhagic fever with renal syndrome in China¶

    Authors: Cheng C et al. | Journal: One Health | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.onehlt.2026.101454
    TL;DR: 中國2005–2022年HFRS呈秋冬與春季雙峰；monthly mean temperature 7.5°C時RR 1.85、relative humidity 38%時RR 1.21，風險受host與環境區域異質性驅動。

    不嘻嘻：HFRS跟天氣一起起伏，老鼠與環境都不放假。

    CEIRR Risk Assessment Pipeline executive reports on H5N1 highly pathogenic avian influenza 2.3.4.4b, swine H1 1B.2, and H9N2 low pathogenicity avian influenza B4.7.2¶

    Authors: | Journal: J Virol | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1128/jvi.00545-26
    TL;DR: CEIRR RAP整合六中心surveillance、phenotypic analysis與computational modeling，評估H5N1 2.3.4.4b、swine H1 1B.2與H9N2 B4.7.2之pandemic potential。

    嘻嘻：influenza風險評估變成流水線，pandemic焦慮稍微有工具。

    AMR in wild animals and plants: global trends and future priorities in wildlife-associated antimicrobial resistance research¶

    Authors: Tipper HJ et al. | Journal: NPJ Antimicrob Resist | Date: 2026 Jun 02
    DOI: 10.1038/s44259-026-00226-3
    TL;DR: 866篇wildlife-associated AMR研究顯示明顯taxonomic與geographic偏差，mammals與birds占多數，plants僅14篇、amphibians 10篇；resistant fungi僅2%，Escherichia占microbial targets 33%。

    不嘻嘻：wildlife AMR研究偏到不行，One Health還在補地圖。

    Metagenomic profiling reveals shared resistome signatures between humans and pigs in Vietnamese smallholder farms¶

    Authors: Linh LTK et al. | Journal: NPJ Antimicrob Resist | Date: 2026 Jun 02
    DOI: 10.1038/s44259-026-00223-6
    TL;DR: 越南50個小農場 paired fecal metagenomics檢出10270個ARGs、550型；人與豬共享52% ARGs，含mcr、blaOXA-58、optrA，並見近緣E. coli strains共享。
