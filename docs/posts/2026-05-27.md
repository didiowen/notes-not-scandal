### Simultaneous enantioselective determination of 30 chiral pesticides in vegetables: enantiomeric composition, separation, and absolute quantification using LC-MS/MS
**Authors**: Zhao T et al. | **Journal**: *Food Chem* | **Date**: 2026-05-22
**DOI**: [10.1016/j.foodchem.2026.149779](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.foodchem.2026.149779)
**TL;DR**: 開發同時定量蔬菜中62種手性農藥對映體的LC-MS/MS方法，55件蔬菜樣品中19件含6種農藥，黃瓜中觀察到1S,3S-bifenthrin選擇性累積。

> 農藥殘留的對映選擇性毒性是常被忽略的食品安全議題，方法學突破值得關注。

### CBC-derived inflammatory indices predict adverse outcomes in geriatric emergency patients with acute infectious diarrhea: a MIMIC-IV analysis

### Multidrug-resistant Escherichia coli and Salmonella spp. in quail eggs from Lima, Peru: Local surveillance and global meta-analysis of prevalence

### Comparative Performance and Species-Specific Recovery Biases of Culture-Based Methods forDetection in Food Products: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis
**Authors**: Meethai C et al. | **Journal**: *Vet Sci* | **Date**: 2026-04-23
**DOI**: [10.3390/vetsci13050415](https://doi.org/10.3390/vetsci13050415)
**TL;DR**: 系統回顧顯示食品中Campylobacter培養法整體敏感度95.8%，但Bolton broth中C. coli回收率比C. jejuni低59.4%，建議高汙染食品基質優先考慮直接培養。

> 培養法的物種偏差可能導致流行病學低估C. coli，食品安全監測需正視這個方法學缺口。

### Biological Detoxification of Mycotoxins by Lactic Acid Bacteria: Safeguarding Food from Fungal Contaminants
**Authors**: Tabassum N et al. | **Journal**: *Toxins (Basel)* | **Date**: 2026-05-20
**DOI**: [10.3390/toxins18050236](https://doi.org/10.3390/toxins18050236)
**TL;DR**: 回顧乳酸菌（LAB）去除黃麴毒素、OTA、DON等主要黴菌毒素的機制，涵蓋細胞壁吸附、酵素生物轉化及抗真菌代謝產物，作為天然食品安全方案具潛力。

> 乳酸菌從發酵食品跨界到黴菌毒素防治，食品微生物學的應用思路令人刮目相看。

### Multi-Mycotoxin Occurrence and Health Risk Assessment in Early Post-Harvest Durum Wheat, Barley, and Maize from Tunisia
**Authors**: Hassouna KB et al. | **Journal**: *Toxins (Basel)* | **Date**: 2026-05-16
**DOI**: [10.3390/toxins18050230](https://doi.org/10.3390/toxins18050230)
**TL;DR**: 突尼西亞小麥、大麥、玉米中多種黴菌毒素共汙染普遍，部分玉米樣本aflatoxin B1超歐盟限量，膳食暴露評估提示對成人存在潛在健康風險。

### Antimicrobial resistance and genomic characteristics of nontyphoidal Salmonella strains from diarrheal patients in Shandong, China
**Authors**: Fan R et al. | **Journal**: *Gut Pathog* | **Date**: 2026-05-25
**DOI**: [10.1186/s13099-026-00840-6](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13099-026-00840-6)
**TL;DR**: 中國山東125株臨床非傷寒Salmonella MDR率59.2%，ST34型最多，全基因組定序揭示63種抗藥基因及171種毒力基因；S. Enteritidis毒力基因最多但抗藥相對較少。
