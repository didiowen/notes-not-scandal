## 🔴 Transplant & Opportunistic Infections

### Clinical and analytical evaluation of a commercial *Aspergillus fumigatus* real-time PCR assay for the diagnosis of invasive pulmonary aspergillosis in patients with hematological malignancies
**Authors**: Gibert C et al. | **Journal**: *J Infect Dis* | **Date**: 2026 May 31
**DOI**: [10.1093/infdis/jiag280](https://doi.org/10.1093/infdis/jiag280)
**TL;DR**: 153 名 hematological malignancies 成人中，*A. fumigatus* qPCR 以 1 次陽性診斷 IPA sensitivity 0.73、specificity 0.71；2 次陽性降至 0.31、升至 0.93，且有 cross-reactivity。

> **不嘻嘻**：PCR 也會眼花，IPA 診斷還是要多帶一點懷疑人生。

### Candidemia in Turkey demonstrates nationwide predominance of non-albicans *Candida* and *Candida auris* detection
**Authors**: Karakoç ZÇ et al. | **Journal**: *Sci Rep* | **Date**: 2026 May 30
**DOI**: [10.1038/s41598-026-53506-3](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-026-53506-3)
**TL;DR**: 土耳其 20 院 398 例 candidemia 中，NAC 占 65.3%，*C. auris* 6.5%；30 天死亡率 56.3%，未移除 CVC 與較高死亡率獨立相關。

> **不嘻嘻**：*Candida* 換班上陣，CVC 還不拔就是幫它加班。

### Analysis of the efficacy and survival of allogeneic hematopoietic stem cell transplantation and nontransplantation treatment in chronic active Epstein-Barr virus infection
**Authors**: Ming X et al. | **Journal**: *Cell Transplant* | **Date**: 2026
**DOI**: [10.1177/09636897261443609](https://doi.org/10.1177/09636897261443609)
**TL;DR**: 75 名 CAEBV 回溯分析顯示，allogeneic HSCT 較 non-HSCT 改善 ORR、3 年 OS 與 EFS；HLH 是較差 survival 的獨立風險因子。

> **嘻嘻**：CAEBV 終於有比較像出口的路，雖然路況仍很硬。

### Molecular detection of zoonotic pathogens in fleas collected from dogs and cats in Portugal

### Trained immunity agonists given to neonatal mice fail to protect against leptospiral infection in adulthood and may cause adverse effects

### Bioinformatics-based B-cell epitope identification from *Leptospira interrogans* outer membrane proteins LIC11574 and LIC13411 for the rational development of a multi-epitope anti-*Leptospira* vaccine
**Authors**: Porcalla JM et al. | **Journal**: *Arch Microbiol* | **Date**: 2026 May 30
**DOI**: [10.1007/s00203-026-04922-6](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00203-026-04922-6)

### Dry Cleaning and Post-Cleaning Strategies in Food Processing Facilities: A Scoping Review
**Authors**: Shahbazi Z et al. | **Journal**: *Compr Rev Food Sci Food Saf* | **Date**: 2026 Jul
**DOI**: [10.1111/1541-4337.70525](https://doi.org/10.1111/1541-4337.70525)
**TL;DR**: 2014–2024 scoping review 納入 91 篇 dry-food facility 清潔研究；cold atmospheric plasma 與 dry steam 在最佳條件下常達 5–7 log CFU microbial reduction。
