    Longitudinal TCR repertoire profiling predicts refractory cytomegalovirus reactivation after haploidentical hematopoietic stem cell transplantation¶

    Authors: Yuan H et al. | Journal: Virology journal | Date: 2026 May 02
    DOI: 10.1186/s12985-026-03181-7
    TL;DR: Haplo-HSCT 後 CMV 反覆活化達 48%；TCR clonality 升高、多樣性下降可預測難治型反應。
    評: 嘻嘻 — 用 TCR 譜系預測誰會被 CMV 纏上，免疫學的算命終於走進臨床。

    Retrospective analysis of the epidemiological characteristics, risk factors, survival rate, and drug resistance of candidemia and construction of a logistic regression prediction model (2013-2024)¶

    The gut metabolite arachidonic acid alleviates intestinal injury induced by a Toxoplasma gondii strain isolated from a wild rodent¶

    Global incidence rates of invasive fusariosis: a systematic review and meta-analysis¶
