    Daily Journal Digest — 2026-06-06¶

    過去 24 小時新增：Transplant ID × 18、One Health × 12、Food Security × 11，共 41 篇。

    🔴 Transplant & Opportunistic Infections¶

    Intracellular acidification by microbiota-derived valeric acid facilitates trans-kingdom ecology limiting Candida parapsilosis colonization¶

    Authors: Yasuma-Mitobe K et al. | Journal: Cell Host Microbe | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1016/j.chom.2026.05.008
    TL;DR: machine learning從Lachnospiraceae metabolomics找出valeric acid、butyric acid可抑制C. parapsilosis；HCT糞便與小鼠資料支持其透過intracellular acidification限制colonization。

    嘻嘻：腸道代謝物又替我們多守一門。

    Egg microbiome of the yellow-spotted Amazon river turtle (Podocnemis unifilis) modulates fusariosis fungal infection and hatching success¶

    Authors: Carranco AS et al. | Journal: Commun Biol | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1038/s42003-026-10404-8
    TL;DR: 黃斑亞馬遜河龜蛋microbiome影響FSSC fusariosis與孵化；mycobiome evenness較低與感染傾向、強度上升相關，microbial diversity較高則與hatching success較佳相關。

    嘻嘻：連龜蛋microbiome都在上感染科。

    Diagnostic accuracy study of the multiplex Truenat MTB Ultima/COVID-19 assay for simultaneous detection of Tuberculosis and SARS-CoV2 (COVID-19)¶

    Authors: Tootla H et al. | Journal: PLOS Glob Public Health | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pgph.0005859
    TL;DR: 1,928名疑似TB成人中，Truenat MTB Ultima/COVID-19偵測TB sensitivity 79.8%、specificity 98.9%；COVID-19 sensitivity僅64.4%、specificity 99.2%，整體表現未達最佳。

    不嘻嘻：multiplex很帥，但臨床還是會皺眉。

    Clinical characteristics and outcomes of invasive fungal infections in critically ill patients with liver cirrhosis¶

    Authors: Seeßle J et al. | Journal: BMC Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13709-5
    TL;DR: 488名ICU liver cirrhosis患者IFI盛行率6.1%，invasive candidiasis與invasive pulmonary aspergillosis各占46.7%、53.3%；IFI顯著降低30與90天存活，ACLF者尤其高風險。

    不嘻嘻：cirrhosis進ICU後真菌很會補刀。

    Survival and predictors of mortality among TB-HIV co-infected children attending ART clinics in public hospitals in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia: a retrospective cohort study¶

    Authors: Habteyes AT et al. | Journal: BMC Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13716-6
    TL;DR: 阿迪斯阿貝巴241名TB-HIV共感染兒童5年存活率83.31%，死亡率14.5%；低CD4、opportunistic infections史、ART adherence不佳分別使死亡風險升高aHR 2.84、5.04、5.69。

    不嘻嘻：兒童TB-HIV照護還是步步驚心。

    A pilot study of the CMV inSIGHT T-cell immunity panel to assess cytomegalovirus (CMV) cell mediated immunity (CMI) in allogeneic hematopoietic cell transplant (HCT) recipients¶

    Authors: Arya S et al. | Journal: Cytotherapy | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jcyt.2026.102878
    TL;DR: 53名接受letermovir的allogeneic HCT成人，D100有73%呈CMV CMI陽性；D100前CMV DNAemia blips與陽性CMV CMI相關，OR 4.89，提示prophylaxis下仍可免疫重建。

    嘻嘻：letermovir時代免疫重建有亮點。

    Antifungal susceptibility of Aspergillus spp. isolated from coffee beans of Los Santos Coffee Region, Costa Rica¶

    Authors: Jaikel-Víquez D et al. | Journal: Front Public Health | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fpubh.2026.1817727
    TL;DR: 哥斯大黎加咖啡豆187株Aspergillus spp.中，A. fumigatus對amphotericin B non-wild type達75%、voriconazole 25%；itraconazole MIC最低且皆為wild type。

    不嘻嘻：咖啡香裡也飄著antifungal壓力。

    Outcomes of concomitant Pneumocystis jirovecii pneumonia and cytomegalovirus co-infection in non-HIV, mechanically ventilated critically ill patients¶

    Authors: Hung WS et al. | Journal: Ann Med | Date: 2026 Dec
    DOI: 10.1080/07853890.2026.2677997
    TL;DR: 132名非HIV、機械通氣PJP ICU患者中，26例合併CMV；CMV在單變項與ICU死亡較高相關，但多變項非獨立因子，死亡主要與ARDS及PJP治療時間相關。

    不嘻嘻：ICU PJP劇本已夠難，CMV還來客串。

    Morphine disrupts antigen-presenting cell function through TLR-4 and autophagy pathways¶

    Authors: Malik JA et al. | Journal: Mol Biol Rep | Date: 2026 Jun 05
    DOI: 10.1007/s11033-026-11785-z
    TL;DR: morphine在macrophages與DCs抑制M. tuberculosis、E. coli等antigen uptake、killing與presentation，並下調MHC I/II、CD80/CD86；機轉涉及TLR-4/NF-κB與autophagy。

    不嘻嘻：morphine連免疫簡報都幫倒忙。

    The clinical profiles and outcomes of patients with herpesviruses in cerebrospinal fluid: 2019 - 2022¶

    Authors: Du Plessis WP et al. | Journal: S Afr Med J | Date: 2026 Jun 02
    DOI: 10.7196/SAMJ.2026.v116i5.3441
    TL;DR: 184名CSF herpesvirus陽性住院者中，HIV占74.5%、EBV占74.5%；僅10.9%被判定為神經症候群病因，院內死亡率21.7%，凸顯CMV治療不足與EBV過度治療問題。

    不嘻嘻：CSF PCR陽性不等於真凶，頭更痛。

    Molecular Identification and Antifungal Susceptibility Patterns of Candida Species in Pediatric Patients at Tabriz Children's Hospital¶

    Authors: Hosseini K et al. | Journal: Biomed Res Int | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1155/bmri/6685542
    TL;DR: 伊朗97株兒科Candida isolates以C. glabrata最多51.5%，67株susceptibility顯示fluconazole與amphotericin B抗藥性普遍；caspofungin對各species仍有97.4–100% susceptibility。

    不嘻嘻：兒科Candida用藥選擇越來越窄。

    Clinical characteristics and predictors of in-hospital mortality in patients with ANCA-associated vasculitis complicated by diffuse alveolar hemorrhage: a retrospective cohort study¶

    Authors: Gao X et al. | Journal: Front Med (Lausanne) | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fmed.2026.1840399
    TL;DR: 58名AAV合併DAH患者院內死亡率22.4%，感染相關septic shock占死亡76.9%；IPA在死亡者達46.2%，但獨立死亡因子為aCCI較高與早期機械通氣。

    不嘻嘻：AAV出血已慘，感染還在旁邊加戲。

    Genotype distribution of Pneumocystis jirovecii in immunocompromised patients: a single-center molecular epidemiology study¶

    Authors: Alencar CS et al. | Journal: Front Fungal Biol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/ffunb.2026.1841693
    TL;DR: 巴西單中心80名疑似PCP免疫低下者中，58份P. jirovecii成功genotyping；genotype 3占69%且跨HIV、solid organ transplant等族群，mixed infections占12%。

    嘻嘻：P. jirovecii分型讓傳播線索更清楚。

    Prevalence and Risk Factors of Latent Tuberculosis Infection among People Living with HIV in Mongolia¶

    Authors: Och N et al. | Journal: Infect Chemother | Date: 2026 Apr 02
    DOI: 10.3947/ic.2025.0136
    TL;DR: 蒙古196名PLHIV無active TB者，LTBI盛行率39.3%；ART中斷與LTBI高度相關，OR 27.7，TB接觸史亦增加風險，支持系統性LTBI screening與preventive therapy。

    不嘻嘻：HIV照護一斷線，TB就準備上場。

    Clinic and Provider Innovations to Improve Tuberculosis Infection Screening and Preventive Therapy Uptake in Brazil: Qualitative Findings From the PREVINE-TB Study¶

    Authors: Kerrigan D et al. | Journal: J Int Assoc Provid AIDS Care | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1177/23259582261447940
    TL;DR: 巴西PREVINE-TB的38場質性訪談指出，nurse預先篩選、specialized TB clinics、TPT Champions與digital communication可提升PLHIV的QFT+使用與TPT uptake。

    嘻嘻：流程設計做好，TPT也能少卡關。

    Interventions to reduce the impact of post-tuberculosis lung disease: a scoping review of the literature¶

    Authors: Byrne AL et al. | Journal: BMC Pulm Med | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1186/s12890-026-04355-3
    TL;DR: scoping review納入87項PTLD介入研究，多數小型且非以PTLD為主要目標；corticosteroids、部分HDT、doxycycline、metformin與inhaled bronchodilators僅呈不一致或初步效益訊號。

    不嘻嘻：PTLD研究很多，答案還是很害羞。

    Global burden of drug-resistant tuberculosis in children: A systematic analysis for the Global Burden of Disease Study¶

    Authors: Liu X et al. | Journal: Eur Respir J | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1183/13993003.01648-2025
    TL;DR: GBD 2021估計2021年全球兒童MDR/RR-TB 32,515例、死亡5,887例，XDR-TB 1,193例、死亡253例；女性、幼齡、低SDI與HIV共感染兒童負擔較重。

    不嘻嘻：兒童MDR/RR-TB完全不是小問題。

    Incidence, risk factors and management of paradoxical reaction in central nervous system tuberculosis in the United Kingdom¶

    Authors: Carter EC et al. | Journal: J Infect | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jinf.2026.106778
    TL;DR: 英國20院229名非HIV CNS TB成人中，21%發生paradoxical reaction，與disability、住院延長、neurosurgical intervention相關；風險因子含CSF M. tuberculosis陽性、重症與CSF neutrophils較高。

    不嘻嘻：CNS TB反轉劇，通常不是好消息。

    🟢 One Health / Zoonoses¶

    Agricultural fungicides shape soil and sediment reservoirs of multidrug-resistant fungi¶

    Authors: Qin Z et al. | Journal: Nat Commun | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.1038/s41467-026-73958-5
    TL;DR: 農業土壤與河川沉積物中>3,900株fungicide-resistant fungi含358株MDR；triazole exposure上調virulence genes與mycotoxin clusters，顯示農業環境可能成為臨床antifungal resistance儲存庫。

    不嘻嘻：農業fungicide也在幫MDR練等。

    The mouse-lethal H5N1 influenza virus carrying the PB2-384L/443R/460M characteristics acquired in avian hosts can effectively utilize human ANP32A/B proteins¶

    Authors: Lin W et al. | Journal: Emerg Microbes Infect | Date: 2026 Jun 05
    DOI: 10.1080/22221751.2026.2685917
    TL;DR: H5N1 PB2-384L/443R/460M可在缺乏PB2-627K或701N下提升mouse lethality，並有效利用human ANP32A/B、增強vRNP互動與assembly，提示禽源演化已具zoonotic風險。

    不嘻嘻：H5N1適應哺乳類的劇情太不浪漫。

    A new clade of H9N2 avian influenza virus circulating in Laos¶

    Authors: Li J et al. | Journal: Emerg Microbes Infect | Date: 2026 Dec
    DOI: 10.1080/22221751.2026.2678641
    TL;DR: 寮國2024年定序52株H9N2，發現BJ/94-like lineage新HA clade 4.6.20，與既有clades抗原反應低；全株帶HA-L226與NP-N52，需持續監測與更新疫苗株。

    不嘻嘻：H9N2又換外套，疫苗追得很喘。

    Metagenomic and serological evidence of emerging tick-borne viruses in livestock, humans, and rats in Pakistan¶

    Authors: Ammar M et al. | Journal: Virol Sin | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1016/j.virs.2026.06.001
    TL;DR: 巴基斯坦87隻livestock-associated ticks RNA sequencing發現至少31種病毒；319 livestock、253 humans、214 rats血清調查顯示JMTV等跨物種暴露，且JMTV neutralizing antibodies證實productive infection。

    不嘻嘻：tick-borne viruses的名單又變厚了。

    Host Ecology Shapes Gut Pathogen Evolution: An Eco-Evolutionary Trade-Off in Plateau Wildlife¶

    Authors: Wang Y et al. | Journal: Environ Microbiol | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1111/1462-2920.70344
    TL;DR: 青藏高原zokor與pika兩年半metagenomics顯示宿主niche塑造gut pathogens、VFGs與PHI genes；zokor豐度與network較高，pika diversity較高但定殖較opportunistic。

    嘻嘻：host ecology把pathogen演化寫得很立體。

    Emergence of Marburg virus disease in Ethiopia: Implications for public health preparedness and its impact on Ethiopia's health system¶

    Authors: Biadgilign S et al. | Journal: PLoS Negl Trop Dis | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pntd.0014394
    TL;DR: 衣索比亞2025年11月首度確認MVD，截至2026-01-25共19例、14死，857名contacts完成21天追蹤；疫情暴露監測、跨境傳播與醫療體系韌性挑戰。

    不嘻嘻：Marburg一出場，公衛壓力直接拉滿。

    Multistate animal-contact-related nontyphoidal Salmonella enterica outbreaks in the United States, 2009-2022: Network and machine learning analyses of exposure sources, settings, and serovars¶

    Authors: Bajwa HUR et al. | Journal: PLoS One | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0344889
    TL;DR: 美國2009–2022年177起跨州動物接觸NTS outbreaks涉及40個serovars；random forest指向鳥類、爬蟲類、農業飼料店與住宅為關鍵暴露來源與場域。

    不嘻嘻：動物接觸outbreak，家門口也很會藏雷。

    A first insight into the diversity of pathogenic Leptospira serogroups in human cases from Ecuador¶

    Authors: Jaramillo NM et al. | Journal: Acta Trop | Date: 2026 Jun 03
    DOI: 10.1016/j.actatropica.2026.108169
    TL;DR: 厄瓜多371名疑似leptospirosis發燒患者中121例MAT陽性，檢出14種Leptospira serogroups；Bataviae、Sejroe、Grippotyphosa最常見，與當地動物reservoirs高度重疊。

    嘻嘻：Leptospira地圖越細，防治越有方向。

    Longitudinal Assessment of Psychological Distress and Coping Strategies in Animal Bite Victims Undergoing Rabies Post-Exposure Prophylaxis¶

    Authors: Singh AD et al. | Journal: Am J Trop Med Hyg | Date: 2026 Jun 04
    DOI: 10.4269/ajtmh.26-0114
    TL;DR: 北印度100名接受rabies PEP的動物咬傷者，day 0到28的IES-R與DASS-21 distress顯著下降；Category III嚴重咬傷、年齡與社經狀態影響anxiety與coping。

    嘻嘻：rabies PEP也需要照顧心理創傷。

    Beyond brain fog: viral proteins as convergent drivers of neuroinflammation and proteinopathy¶

    Authors: Käufer C et al. | Journal: Curr Opin Virol | Date: 2026 Jun 05
    DOI: 10.1016/j.coviro.2026.101559
    TL;DR: post-viral neurological syndromes可能由shed viral proteins驅動，而非CNS大量複製；多種病毒蛋白收斂活化glial TLR4/TLR2與破壞proteostasis，形成neuroinflammation與proteinopathy。

    不嘻嘻：post-viral腦霧可能比想像更黏人。

    Integrative phenotypic and functional genomic characterization of virulence and antimicrobial resistance in Salmonella enterica isolates from reptiles¶

    Authors: Satharasinghe DA et al. | Journal: Front Microbiol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fmicb.2026.1841627
    TL;DR: 佛羅里達爬蟲類Salmonella culture陽性率16.41%，24株S. enterica中58.3%為MDR，WGS檢出55個AMR genes與239個VF genes，支持爬蟲為mobile genetic elements儲存庫。

    不嘻嘻：爬蟲身上的Salmonella很有武裝感。

    Seasonal surveillance of Salmonella prevalence, antimicrobial resistance, and genetic relatedness in chickens from slaughterhouses and retail markets in Northeast Thailand¶

    Authors: Zhang Z et al. | Journal: Vet World | Date: 2026 Apr
