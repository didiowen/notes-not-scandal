    AIDS patients with Talaromycosis Marneffei exhibit inflammatory activation and depletion in their peripheral blood monocytes¶

    Authors: Guo P et al. | Journal: PLoS Negl Trop Dis | Date: 2026 May 04
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pntd.0014306

    Feasibility, accuracy, and effect of a rapid point-of-care serological test (SeroSelectTB) to identify presumptive pulmonary TB patients for confirmatory testing in Ethiopia, South Africa, and Tanzania: a multicenter, open-label, parallel-group, randomized, controlled trial¶

    評: 嘻嘻：基層診所 POC TB 試劑成真，治療延遲又少一道關卡。

    Efficacy, safety, and completion of modified short-course rifapentine and isoniazid for latent tuberculosis infection in patients with high-risk rheumatic disease: a multicentre, open-label, randomized, non-inferiority trial¶
