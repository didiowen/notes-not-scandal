    Non-typhoid Salmonella Vascular Infection¶

    Chen-NTSVI score [@Chen2012simplescoring]¶

    RIsk factors Point
    male sex +1
    hypertension +1
    coronary arterial disease +1
    serogroup C1 +1
    immunosuppressive therapy -1
    malignancy -1

    ≥ +1 → arrange CT
    Se: 95.0%
    Sp: 45.3%
    AUC: 0.83 (0.78-0.89)

    TTP-NTSVI score [@Lin2018Utilityblood]¶

    RIsk factors Point
    male sex +6
    PAD +4
    coronary arterial disease +3
    TTP < 10h +3

    ≥ +7 → arrange CT
    Se: 70.6%
    Sp: 82.5%
    AUC: 0.851
