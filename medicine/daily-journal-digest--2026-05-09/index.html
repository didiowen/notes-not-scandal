    🟢 One Health / Zoonoses¶

    The wild, wild west of plasmids: First insights into comparative genomics of Borrelia burgdorferi sensu lato¶

    Authors: Hepner S et al. | Journal: PLoS One | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0346097
    TL;DR: Borrelia burgdorferi s.l. 86株比較：質體高度可變，鼠類宿主質體數較多
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 萊姆螺旋體把基因放在一堆會自由組合的質體裡，難怪一個世紀都搞不定。

    First Nationwide Assessment of Knowledge, Attitudes, and Practices Related to Tick-borne Diseases among Livestock Farmers in Bangladesh¶

    Authors: Ahmed MJ et al. | Journal: Acta Trop | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.actatropica.2026.108126
    TL;DR: 孟加拉451位畜農中僅4.66%對蜱媒疾病知識足夠，21%知會人傳人
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 教育落差讓蜱媒病在源頭就破口，光靠藥浴不會贏。

    Challenges, barriers and opportunities for neglected tropical disease programs of conflict-affected African countries¶

    Authors: Harding-Esch EM et al. | Journal: Int Health | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1093/inthealth/ihaf149
    TL;DR: 衝突影響非洲NTD計畫面臨進入、社區、永續、財源等多重複合困境
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 戰亂裡的被忽略熱帶病再被忽略一次，2030 NTD目標越走越遠。

    Armadillo meat consumption and Hansen's disease (leprosy): a case-control study in Brazil¶

    Authors: Repsold TAR et al. | Journal: Trans R Soc Trop Med Hyg | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1093/trstmh/trag054
    TL;DR: 巴西病例對照：食用犰狳肉與痲瘋病風險aOR達16.5，支持人畜共通傳播
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 犰狳肉真的會吃出痲瘋，美洲One Health監測該動了。

    Baylisascaris sp. (Nematoda: Ascaridae) in a Wild Specimen of Procyon cancrivorus (Cuvier, 1798) (Carnivora: Procyonidae) in Paraguay¶

    Authors: Vetter JR et al. | Journal: J Wildl Dis | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.7589/JWD-D-25-00184
    TL;DR: 巴拉圭首例南美浣熊驗出Baylisascaris樣蟲卵，提示新興人畜共通風險
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 浣熊蛔蟲在南美洲也報到，神經型幼蟲移行又多一片地圖。

    Next-generation sequencing in tick-borne apicomplexan parasites: approaches for Theileria and Babesia, a systematic review¶

    Authors: Mukandabvute D et al. | Journal: Trop Anim Health Prod | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1007/s11250-026-05056-z
    TL;DR: 系統回顧127篇 Theileria/Babesia定序研究，Nanopore直接RNA為新興方向
    評: 嘻嘻 — 老牌寄生蟲被新定序技術重新拆解，疫苗靶點地圖逐漸清楚。

    Assessing antimicrobial resistance in Shiga toxin-producing Escherichia coli O157 isolates from human clinical cases in the United States, 2010-2021¶

    Authors: Bhatt T et al. | Journal: Future Microbiol | Date: 2026 May 09
    DOI: 10.1080/17460913.2026.2671606
    TL;DR: 美國STEC O157人類分離株2010-2021年tetracycline與SXT抗藥率持續上升
    評: 不嘻嘻 — STEC不該用抗生素治療卻越來越抗藥，源頭顯然不在診間。

    Molecular detection and characterization of Anaplasma phagocytophilum-related strains and Hepatozoon spp. in domestic cats in eastern Türkiye¶

    Authors: Akgul O et al. | Journal: BMC Vet Res | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1186/s12917-026-05542-4
    TL;DR: 土耳其東部家貓78.5%驗出Anaplasma phagocytophilum樣株AP-like 1
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 家貓帶蜱媒病原比例高得驚人，獸醫該開始把貓也納入監測。

    Medium- and Large-sized Mammals on the Plateau: An Evolutionary Crucible for Zoonotic Pathogens - Ecological Drivers, Adaptive Evolution, and One Health Control¶

    Authors: Tan S et al. | Journal: Microb Ecol | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1007/s00248-026-02772-9
    TL;DR: 中國四大高原中大型哺乳類為人畜共通病演化熱區，回顧呼籲One Health整合
    評: 嘻嘻 — 高原生態被當作病原演化坩堝來分析，視角夠新鮮。

    Review of Right Heart Failure¶

    Authors: Neary JM | Journal: Vet Clin North Am Food Anim Pract | Date: 2026 May 07
    DOI: 10.1016/j.cvfa.2026.03.002
    TL;DR: 牛右心衰竭回顧聚焦高海拔疾病與BCHF，預防仍仰賴遺傳選育
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 牛的右心衰竭只能靠選育預防，臨床上基本沒招。

    🟡 Food Security / Food Safety¶

    Seeding food security: Overcoming barriers to quality potato seed adoption among smallholders in Kenya¶

    Authors: Mwangi MN et al. | Journal: PLoS One | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0346796
    TL;DR: 肯亞44%小農採用優質馬鈴薯種，可提升家戶飲食多樣性與食物消費分數
    評: 嘻嘻 — 一顆好種子真的能餵飽一家，政策該把通路打開。

    Swedish veterinarians' perspectives on the work with Emergency Animal Diseases: part 2 - Crucial relationships and the position in-between¶

    Authors: Gröndal H et al. | Journal: Prev Vet Med | Date: 2026 May 01
    DOI: 10.1016/j.prevetmed.2026.106910
    TL;DR: 瑞典臨床獸醫處理緊急動物疫病高度仰賴與農民信任關係
    評: 嘻嘻 — 證實「關係勞動」也是疫病防治硬實力，量化獸醫圈該認真看。

    Evaluation of PCR-Based H5N1 Influenza Detection Methods in Milk from an Inter-laboratory Comparison Study Demonstrating Method-Dependent Sensitivity Variability¶

    Authors: Miller MR et al. | Journal: J Food Prot | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jfp.2026.100798
    TL;DR: 牛奶H5N1 PCR實驗室間比對顯示低濃度檢出能力差異大，方法選擇影響敏感度
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 奶裡有H5N1卻有四分之三實驗室在低拷貝量驗不出，食安監測要重練。

    Mechanistically Informed and Omics-Guided Essential Oil Applications for Food Preservation¶

    Authors: Islam Bhuiyan MN et al. | Journal: J Food Prot | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jfp.2026.100806
    TL;DR: 精油食品保鮮回顧整合機制學、多體學與AI建模，仍待標準化與真實食品驗證
    評: 嘻嘻 — 終於有人把精油從「替代醫學味」拉到組學驗證，乾淨標籤不再憑感覺。

    Marine Nematodes in Seafood: A Review of Food Safety Concerns, Advanced Detection Techniques, and Mitigation Strategies¶

    Authors: Ye X et al. | Journal: J Food Prot | Date: 2026 May 05
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jfp.2026.100803
    TL;DR: 海產線蟲回顧聚焦Anisakis與Pseudoterranova，HSI與分子檢測為新興工具
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 你以為的生魚片風險只是冰山一角，凍得不夠就靠運氣。

    Rapid and Simultaneous Detection of Five Classical Staphylococcus aureus Enterotoxin Genes via Multiplex Melting Curve Analysis Using Self-Quenching Primers¶

    Authors: Liu J et al. | Journal: J Food Prot | Date: 2026 May 05
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jfp.2026.100804
    TL;DR: 自淬熔解曲線多重PCR可同步檢出五種SE基因，乳源S. aureus 47.95%帶毒
    評: 嘻嘻 — 一管反應五個毒素，乳廠日常監測終於不用再開五張單。

    The Secondary Colonizer Hypothesis: an Ecological View of Listeria monocytogenes Persistence in Food Processing Environments¶

    Authors: Burnett J et al. | Journal: J Food Prot | Date: 2026 May 05
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jfp.2026.100801
    TL;DR: 提出Listeria在Gram陰性生物膜空隙作為次要殖民者，Veillonella為橋接物種
    評: 嘻嘻 — Listeria原來是看別人留下來的廚餘填房，清潔策略要改打生物膜消長。

    Orobanche cumana KAI2d2 mediates perception of dehydrocostus lactone and strigolactones and is inhibited by triazole ureas¶

    DOI: 10.1016/j.xplc.2026.101889
    TL;DR: 列當KAI2d2感知strigolactones與dehydrocostus，三氮唑脲可抑制萌發
    評: 嘻嘻 — 寄生雜草終於有針對性開關，向日葵不用陪葬。

    Real-time analytical insights for disease surveillance and response during the severe drought and food security crisis, Somalia 2022-2023¶

    Authors: Polonsky JA et al. | Journal: Confl Health | Date: 2026 May 09
    DOI: 10.1186/s13031-026-00799-w
    TL;DR: 索馬利亞乾旱期間WHO-SIDE平台以Rt即時估計與情境投影支援疫病監測
    評: 嘻嘻 — 在資源最緊的地方做最即時的疫情建模，工具實用就值得擴散。
