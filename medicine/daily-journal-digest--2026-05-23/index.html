    Differences in tuberculosis prevalence by sex in low- and middle-income countries over 1993-2025: A systematic review and meta-analysis¶

    Authors: Swartwood NA et al. | Journal: PLoS Med | Date: 2026 May 22
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pmed.1005114
    TL;DR: 30年系統性文獻回顧顯示LMICs結核病男:女感染比持續維持2.02，性別差距未縮小。

    不嘻嘻：三十年過去了，M:F=2.02，這個比例根本比某些機構的升遷比例還穩。

    Human cytomegalovirus terminase inhibitors letermovir, tomeglovir, and the halogenated benzimidazoles modify DNA cleavage/packaging to generate a super-genomic DNA species by cleavage site skipping¶

    Authors: Ourahmane A et al. | Journal: Antiviral Res | Date: 2026 May 20
    DOI: 10.1016/j.antiviral.2026.106446
    TL;DR: Letermovir、tomeglovir、BDCRB均透過裂解位點跳過誘導HCMV產生M+ supergenomic DNA，揭示保守的抗藥機制。

    不嘻嘻：三種作用機制不同的藥物一起誘發同一個逃脫路徑，HCMV的求生意志令人肅然起敬。

    Emerging evidence for anti-PD-1 and IFN-γ as adjunctive immunotherapy in invasive mold infections¶

    Authors: Serris A et al. | Journal: mBio | Date: 2026 May 22
    DOI: 10.1128/mbio.00230-26
    TL;DR: 8名難治侵犯性黴菌感染患者以anti-PD-1加IFN-γ免疫輔助治療，6例存活，中位追蹤8.5個月。

    嘻嘻：免疫輔助治療讓難治黴菌感染8人存活6人，感染科終於也能說「我們有新武器了」。

    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pgph.0006327
    TL;DR: 遠距眼底攝影篩查PLHIV的CMV視網膜炎，靈敏度87%、特異度98%，可取代傳統裂隙燈眼底鏡。

    嘻嘻：靈敏度87%、特異度98%，這台相機幾乎比某些眼底鏡判讀者還可靠，而且可以遠距。
