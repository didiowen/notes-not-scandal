    Oral Wash PCR Improves the Diagnosis of Pneumocystis Pneumonia in Immunocompromised Patients Without HIV: A Prospective Multicenter Study¶

    Authors: Robert-Gangneux F et al. | Journal: Clin Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun 11
    DOI: 10.1093/cid/ciag356

    TL;DR: HIV-negative免疫低下369例中76例PCP；OW PCR陽性率81.6%，7 copies/mL門檻PPV 90%、敏感度73.5%，BDG+/OW PCR+之PPV達92%。

    嘻嘻：漱口也能抓PCP，少一點侵入就多一點人性。
