    Mucorales PCR for the rapid diagnosis of mucormycosis: 6 years of testing in a national reference laboratory and tertiary hospital¶

    Authors: Haas AL et al. | Journal: J Clin Microbiol | Date: 2026 Jun 12
    DOI: 10.1128/jcm.01911-25
    TL;DR: Mucorales PCR 6 年 5,779 次檢測陽性率 2.9%，較 culture 早約 7 天；fresh tissue 診斷率最高，特異性 99%，serum/CSF 偽陰性較多。

    嘻嘻：Mucorales 終於能快點現形，刀下去前多一點底氣。

    People with prior tuberculosis have accelerated frailty and differential survival into older age: a cross-sectional analysis in Southern Africa¶

    Authors: Coleman M et al. | Journal: J Infect Dis | Date: 2026 Jun 12
    DOI: 10.1093/infdis/jiag299
    TL;DR: 南非、辛巴威 ≥40 歲族群中，曾有 tuberculosis 者 frailty 勝算加倍（OR 2.06, 95%CI 1.38–3.03），且存活至高齡比例較差。

    不嘻嘻：tuberculosis 退場後還補一刀，人生好難。

    不嘻嘻：PJP 總愛晚點名，腎移植門診真的不能鬆。
