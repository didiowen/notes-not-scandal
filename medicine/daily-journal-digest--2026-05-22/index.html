    Daily Journal Digest — 2026-05-22¶

    過去 24 小時新增：Transplant ID × 12、One Health × 12、Food Security × 10，共 34 篇。

    ⭐ 今日亮點¶

    • Global approaches to infectious disease surveillance and modeling
      Nature Medicine綜述首次系統評估federated learning突破全球傳染病資料壁壘的可行性，架構未來疫情應對基礎。
    • Discovery of novel triazine thiosemicarbazide derivatives as potential synergists for combating invasive candidiasis
      化合物A21以FICI 0.002協同fluconazole對抗藥性Candida，可能逆轉目前azole抗藥困境，臨床轉化潛力高。
    • Prevalence of HIV-associated histoplasmosis in the Guiana Shield: a prospective study
      前瞻性研究確認HIV患者histoplasmosis盛行率達13.8%，凸顯熱帶地區伺機性感染診斷缺口的緊迫性。
    • A SAUR gene enhances maize drought resilience by promoting silk elongation
      Nature克隆出控制玉米乾旱抗性的qDR9基因，直接影響全球糧食安全的抗旱育種策略。
    • Local heterogeneity in Lassa fever serology in rural Nigeria: Implications for vaccine trial site selection
      村級seroprevalence異質性數據為Lassa fever疫苗試驗精準選點提供科學依據，加速疫苗開發進程。

    🔴 Transplant & Opportunistic Infections¶

    Germlings of Rhizopus arrhizus, rather than sporangiospores, lead to rhino-orbito-cerebral mucormycosis acquired intranasally in a murine model of uncontrolled diabetes¶

    Authors: Singh R et al. | Journal: Microb Pathog | Date: 2026-05-19
    DOI: 10.1016/j.micpath.2026.108577
    TL;DR: 研究以糖尿病小鼠建立鼻腔感染模型，證實Rhizopus arrhizus的germlings（非sporangiospores）才能成功引發鼻眶腦型mucormycosis。

    嘻嘻！搞清楚是germlings在作怪，建立mucormycosis動物模型的關鍵謎題終於解開了。

    Case report from a pediatric multidrug-resistant tuberculosis case with Tsukamurella co-infection identified by whole-genome sequencing: a diagnostic and therapeutic challenge¶

    Authors: Abebaw Y et al. | Journal: BMC Infect Dis | Date: 2026-05-20
    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13524-y
    TL;DR: 兒童MDR-TB患者以whole-genome sequencing確診合併Tsukamurella co-infection，凸顯WGS在診斷複雜抗藥感染中的不可替代性。

    不嘻嘻。MDR-TB加上Tsukamurella的combo，沒有WGS根本看不穿。

    Prevalence of HIV-associated histoplasmosis in the Guiana Shield: a prospective study¶

    Authors: Françoise U et al. | Journal: Int J Infect Dis | Date: 2026-05-19
    DOI: 10.1016/j.ijid.2026.108795
    TL;DR: Guiana Shield地區HIV陽性發燒住院者中histoplasmosis盛行率高達13.8%，是最常見的伺機性感染之一，但當地缺乏常規抗原檢測。

    不嘻嘻。CD4低的病人裡histoplasmosis比TB還常見，但當地連抗原試劑都沒有。

    Diagnostic Performance and Resource Utilization of Combining Blood Gene Expression, Cell-Free DNA, and Urine Chemokines for Monitoring Kidney Rejection¶

    Authors: Singla A et al. | Journal: Clin J Am Soc Nephrol | Date: 2026-05-21
    DOI: 10.2215/CJN.0000001075

    嘻嘻！尿液便宜做門衛、dd-cfDNA只給高風險者，排斥監測的效益與成本同步優化。

    不嘻嘻。多壓一道belatacept換來感染暴增，排斥與感染之間的天平又更難平衡了。

    嘻嘻！FICI低到0.002讓fluconazole起死回生，這個新化合物或許是對抗抗藥性念珠菌的秘密武器。

    Authors: Molinari C et al. | Journal: Virulence | Date: 2026-05-21
