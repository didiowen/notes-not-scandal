    Artesunate repurposing for cytomegalovirus management: a systematic review of efficacy, mechanisms, and perspectives¶

    Authors: Zhang H et al. | Journal: Front Cell Infect Microbiol | Date: 2026-05-07
    DOI: 10.3389/fcimb.2026.1815679
    TL;DR: Artesunate系統性回顧（46篇）顯示對CMV有效且無交叉抗藥性，但半衰期短、臨床證據仍不足，需更多隨機試驗佐證其移植後應用。

    嘻嘻：—瘧疾藥打CMV的瘋狂點子，居然看起來有點道理！瘋狂科學家能量。

    Invasive Fusariosis: A Retrospective Case Series Evaluating In Vitro Minimum Inhibitory Concentrations and Clinical Response¶

    Authors: Kovac V et al. | Journal: Open Forum Infect Dis | Date: 2026-05-07
    DOI: 10.1093/ofid/ofag248
    TL;DR: 21例侵襲性fusariosis回顧：94%三唑類MIC≥4 μg/mL，但triazole±terbinafine仍達76%十二週存活率，說明體外MIC高不等於臨床失敗。

    不嘻嘻：—MIC幾乎全部破表但還能活，感覺在跟抗黴菌治療的物理定律對賭，膽子很大。

    PCR Fluorescent Probe-based Detection of Aspergillus Spp., Cryptococcus neoformans, and Pneumocystis jirovecii¶

    Authors: Zou W et al. | Journal: J Vis Exp | Date: 2026-05-08
    DOI: 10.3791/68819
    TL;DR: 發表一套痰液多重螢光PCR流程可同步偵測Aspergillus、Cryptococcus neoformans及Pneumocystis jirovecii，附標準操作步驟與判讀閾值，適合臨床推廣。

    嘻嘻：—三個強敵一管搞定，臨床診斷的省時神器！

    Early Posttransplant Lymphoproliferative Disorder and Cryptosporidiosis After Liver Transplantation¶

    Authors: Hakamifard A et al. | Journal: Case Rep Med | Date: 2026-05-23
    DOI: 10.1155/carm/5215414
    TL;DR: 肝臟移植後同時發生Cryptosporidium腸炎與早發性PTLD（彌漫大B細胞淋巴瘤），rituximab後仍死亡，提醒移植後持續腹瀉需納入PTLD鑑別診斷。

    不嘻嘻：—一個病人把感染科和血液腫瘤科的噩夢同時集滿，太不公平了。

    Neurological manifestations in Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome: a systematic review¶

    Authors: Raccagni NG et al. | Journal: Front Immunol | Date: 2026-05-07
    DOI: 10.3389/fimmu.2026.1829058
    TL;DR: WAS系統性回顧32例：神經病變分出血、免疫媒介、感染（JC virus/PML為主）及腫瘤（CNS淋巴瘤/PTLD）四類，整體死亡率59.4%，感染及腫瘤型預後最差。

    不嘻嘻：—WAS神經問題分四路夾擊且每路都要命，這篇算是罕見疾病的悲傷全景圖。

    Dynamic Reprogramming of Fungal Cell Walls Underlies Germination and Immune Exposure in Zygomycetous Fungal Pathogens¶

    Authors: Gautam I et al. | Journal: bioRxiv | Date: 2026-05-17
    DOI: 10.64898/2026.05.12.724644
    TL;DR: 毛黴菌萌發過程中細胞壁由β-1,3-glucan轉為chitin-chitosan為主，此重塑強化中性球辨識，為mucormycosis免疫逃脫機制研究提供分子基礎。

    嘻嘻：—基礎科學找到抗黴新靶點，細胞壁重塑機制被抓包，值得期待後續藥物開發！

    Solitary Cavitary Right Upper Lobe Lesion Revealing Disseminated Cryptococcosis in a Kidney Transplant Recipient: A Diagnostic Challenge¶

    不嘻嘻：—腫瘤？其實是黴菌！這種診斷反轉在免疫抑制患者身上真的讓人不得不提高警覺。

    Pulmonary Fungal Infections in Immunocompetent Patients¶

    不嘻嘻：—免疫正常也會得黴菌感染還長得跟肺癌一樣，診斷科的折磨永無止境。

    Surgical and Endovascular Management of Pseudoaneurysms of the Ascending Aorta and Aortic Root: Operative Strategies and Long-Term Outcomes¶

    Authors: Abu Ghannam R et al. | Journal: Aorta (Stamford) | Date: 2026-05-24
    DOI: 10.1055/a-2875-3047
    TL;DR: 12例升主動脈假性動脈瘤回顧，41.6%為感染性，住院死亡率8.3%，一例晚期死於candidemia合併復發感染；外科仍為主要處置，高危患者可考慮血管內治療。

    不嘻嘻：—感染加主動脈問題，外科已盡力，candidemia還是最後補刀，唉。

    The risk of venous thromboembolism in kidney transplant recipients is enhanced following a cytomegalovirus infection¶

    Authors: Masset C et al. | Journal: Front Immunol | Date: 2026-05-08
    DOI: 10.3389/fimmu.2026.1816608

    嘻嘻：—CMV會造成血栓終於有大型隊列數字支持，以後跟病人解釋CMV有更多說服力了！

    Clinical and computed tomography (CT) features of nodular pulmonary histoplasmosis¶

    不嘻嘻：—長得跟肺癌一模一樣，非地方性流行區的醫師每次診斷都要備受考驗。

    Targeted Tuberculosis (TB) Vaccination Strategies in the United States: A Modeling Study¶

    DOI: 10.64898/2026.05.11.26352914

    嘻嘻：—TB疫苗快來了，建模說針對高風險族群可以砍掉一半的病例，太令人期待！

    HOW TO INTRODUCE A NEW TB VACCINE IN ADOLESCENTS AND ADULTS: Insights from Key Stakeholders in Mozambique, Southern Africa¶

    嘻嘻：—田野調查告訴我們如何實際推疫苗，這種接地氣的公衛研究最務實可愛！

    Number Needed to Vaccinate with a Novel Tuberculosis Vaccine to Prevent Tuberculosis in High-Risk Populations, United States¶

    嘻嘻：—NNV 217非常划算，針對HIV族群優先打TB疫苗是最CP值的做法！

    不嘻嘻：—3個月大嬰兒、腸結核、先天免疫缺陷……每個字都令人心疼，這個案例很沉重。

    Metabolomics and Artificial Intelligence (AI) assisted metabolomics in the diagnosis of non-tuberculous mycobacterial infections: progress and challenges¶

    嘻嘻：—AI加入NTM診斷戰場，萬一能找到那個怎麼培養都不出來的菌，就太讚了！

    不嘻嘻：—黃麴毒素從農田污染一路進到牛奶裡，食品安全鏈每個環節都有漏洞。

    Human-animal contact and zoonotic exposure from wild and domestic animals: A cross-sectional study in wildlife-rich areas of Bolivia, Chile, and Guatemala¶

    不嘻嘻：—幾乎每個人都在接觸動物，但幾乎沒人知道那暗藏什麼風險，公衛教育缺口很大。

    Prevalence and antimicrobial susceptibility ofisolated from dairy goats in Shaanxi, China, with genomic characterization of a multidrug-resistant subset¶

    不嘻嘻：—MRSA ST59和ST398出現在山羊奶裡，抗藥Staph無所不在，One Health的警訊很清楚。

    Seasonal and spatial dynamics ofand antimicrobial resistance in a one health dairy ecosystem in Western Canada¶

    嘻嘻：—乳牛場的Enterococcus沒有大量外流到廢水系統，比預期的乾淨一些，算是個好消息！

    Transmission of severe fever with thrombocytopenia syndrome virus from a dog to humans and a cat in a household cluster¶

    不嘻嘻：—狗、主人、貓全家一起被SFTS攻陷，伴侶動物的傳播風險真的不能再被輕忽！

    The First Global Forum of WHO Collaborating Centres and the One Health Summit, a landmark event redefining the future of global health science, with a reviewed history and update of the One Health concept¶

    Seroincidence of  Brucella spp. infection among humans and livestock in Northern Kenya¶

    Authors: Mwatondo A et al. | Journal: Sci Rep | Date: 2026-05-24
