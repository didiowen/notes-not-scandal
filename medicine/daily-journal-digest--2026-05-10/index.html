    Daily Journal Digest — 2026-05-10¶

    過去 24 小時新增：Transplant ID × 0、One Health × 10、Food Security × 10，共 20 篇。

    🔴 Transplant & Opportunistic Infections¶

    今日無符合條件的新文章。

    🟢 One Health / Zoonoses¶

    Interfacial work function matching enables efficient hydrogen spillover for superior alkaline hydrogen evolution¶

    Authors: Sun Z et al. | Journal: J Adv Res | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jare.2026.04.070
    TL;DR: NixRuy@Cu 催化劑透過界面功函數調控達高效鹼性電解水產氫
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 演算法把材料電化學論文塞進 One Health 區，看來 PubMed 也搞不清自己的本業。

    Detoxification of antibiotic pollution using nanoparticle systems: Introspecting the mechanisms, current status and emerging trends¶

    Authors: Kannan EP et al. | Journal: Environ Res | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.envres.2026.124683
    TL;DR: 奈米材料可降解環境抗生素殘留，有望緩解 AMR 與 ARG 擴散
    評: 嘻嘻 — 用奈米材料對抗 AMR，至少這條戰線還有人在想新點子。

    Expression dynamics and antiviral role of chicken OASL during infectious bronchitis virus infection¶

    Authors: Chen Y et al. | Journal: Poult Sci | Date: 2026 Apr 27
    DOI: 10.1016/j.psj.2026.107014
    TL;DR: 雞 OASL 受 IBV 誘導表達並抑制病毒複製，提供抗病毒新標的
    評: 嘻嘻 — 雞沒有 RIG-I 還能靠 OASL 撐場面，禽免疫學總算有一點好消息。

    Government intervention and carbon welfare performance: Nonlinear effects and spatial spillovers from China¶

    Authors: Zhang Z et al. | Journal: J Environ Manage | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jenvman.2026.129871
    TL;DR: 中國省級政府干預與碳福利績效呈 U 型關係，過閾值才有正向效果
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 把「政府要管到一定程度才有用」寫成 U 型曲線，套套詞 dressed up as 空間計量。

    Genomic insights into multidrug-resistant bacteria: Emergence of mcr-1.1 in Escherichia coli, blaKPC-2 in Klebsiella pneumoniae and Serratia marcescens from clinical settings¶

    Authors: Hameed MF et al. | Journal: Virulence | Date: 2026 Dec
    DOI: 10.1080/21505594.2026.2668179
    TL;DR: 安徽臨床分離株同時帶 mcr-1.1 與 blaKPC-2，跨物種抗藥組合警訊
    評: 不嘻嘻 — colistin 與 carbapenem 抗性同框出現，臨床最後一道牆又裂了一條縫。

    Elucidating the regulatory role of the Shine-Dalgarno sequence in structural gene expression of the malolactic enzyme operon using a GFP/mCherry model¶

    Authors: Liu Z et al. | Journal: World J Microbiol Biotechnol | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1007/s11274-026-04999-7
    TL;DR: SD2 序列在偶聯 operon 顯示架構特異、非線性的轉譯調控
    評: 嘻嘻 — 為了釀酒乳酸菌做 SD 序列工程，連葡萄酒風味都能合成生物學化。

    Accelerated Tandem Catalysis for Industrial-Level Nitric Oxide Electroreduction to Ammonia¶

    Authors: Zhang YC et al. | Journal: Angew Chem Int Ed Engl | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1002/anie.5660280
    TL;DR: Co3O4-x/Cu 串聯催化以 94.9% FE 將 NO 電還原為 NH3
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 又是材料化學亂入 One Health，FE 雖漂亮但跟一個健康沾不上邊。

    Counting cases, conserving species: addressing highly pathogenic avian influenza in wildlife¶

    Authors: Knief U et al. | Journal: Biol Rev Camb Philos Soc | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1002/brv.70173
    TL;DR: H5N1 panzootic 重創野鳥族群，亟需整合死亡、血清與基因監測
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 高病原禽流感從季節疫情變全球常駐，野鳥族群的喪鐘越敲越響。

    Evolution and transmission landscape of the staphylococcal msrA gene mediating resistance to 14-membered macrolides and type B streptogramins¶

    Authors: Janelidze D et al. | Journal: Front Microbiol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fmicb.2026.1815688
    TL;DR: 葡萄球菌 msrA 受純化選擇保守、靠質體在物種間水平傳播
    評: 不嘻嘻 — S. aureus 又被點名為抗藥基因的中央車站，巨環內酯類版圖再縮一塊。

    Dynamics of microbial contamination and hygiene risk points in the production of ready-to-eat Yao meat¶

    Authors: Liu M et al. | Journal: Front Microbiol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fmicb.2026.1829503
    TL;DR: 即食搖肉解凍與揉製階段污染最高，包裝段顯示衛生管控漏洞
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 即食肉品從解凍那一刻就輸了，包裝段再補一槍，HACCP 看了會嘆氣。

    🟡 Food Security / Food Safety¶

    Polycationic nanopeptide-fused endolysins for the control of Mannheimia haemolytica¶

    Authors: Moqaddes S et al. | Journal: Appl Microbiol Biotechnol | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1007/s00253-026-13835-9
    TL;DR: PCNP-185 endolysin 對 M. haemolytica 達 4.5 log 殺菌
    評: 嘻嘻 — 噬菌體 endolysin 配陽離子小肽再加螯合劑，又一個替抗生素紓壓的工程方案。

    Wearable Plant Electronics Enables Early Detection of Salt Stress by Tracking K+/Na+ Homeostasis and Salicylic Acid Accumulation¶

    Authors: Chen H et al. | Journal: Anal Chem | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1021/acs.analchem.6c00860
    TL;DR: 穿戴式 LIG 電極可在 24 h 內偵測植物 K+/Na+ 與水楊酸鹽逆境動態
    評: 嘻嘻 — 植物也能戴智慧手環，鹽害早期警報系統聽起來很賽博龐克。

    Pyruvylation: a new post-translational modification mechanism¶

    Authors: Guo W et al. | Journal: Trends Endocrinol Metab | Date: 2026 May 07
    DOI: 10.1016/j.tem.2026.04.010
    TL;DR: STAT1 pyruvylation 為新型 PTM，可抑制 IFN-I 抗病毒訊號
    評: 嘻嘻 — 翻譯後修飾家族再添丙酮酸成員，代謝物與抗病毒免疫的對話又多一層。

    Genome-wide association and functional studies reveal the genetic basis of salt tolerance in naked barley¶

    Authors: Sreesaeng J et al. | Journal: J Adv Res | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jare.2026.05.016
    TL;DR: GWAS 鎖定 HvCML16、HvBGL10、HvWAKL2 為裸大麥耐鹽育種候選基因
    評: 嘻嘻 — 裸大麥也加入耐鹽明星陣容，糧食抗逆育種多了三張底牌。

    Urban-rural and regional inequalities in PCDD/F exposure across China's urban agglomerations: Insights from a multimedia food-chain model with cooking bioaccessibility¶

    Authors: Li Y et al. | Journal: Environ Pollut | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.envpol.2026.128288
    TL;DR: 中國城市戴奧辛膳食暴露為農村 1.92–6.51 倍，2–5 歲幼兒風險最高
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 戴奧辛吃進嘴裡都市勝鄉村，幼兒首當其衝，幾十年的工業遺產還在收費。

    Detection challenges of Escherichia coli O157 in raw meat: Diagnostic discordance between VIDAS, qPCR, and culture-based methods¶

    Authors: Soycan A et al. | Journal: Diagn Microbiol Infect Dis | Date: 2026 May 01
    DOI: 10.1016/j.diagmicrobio.2026.117446
    TL;DR: 生肉 E. coli O157：qPCR 8%、VIDAS 4.7%、培養 3.3%，三法落差大
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 三法陽性率天差地遠，光靠 stx 基因會漏掉帶 eae 的真兇。

    Dual-emission carbon dots for sensitive detection of sulfamethoxazole in milk samples¶

    Authors: Zou R et al. | Journal: Spectrochim Acta A Mol Biomol Spectrosc | Date: 2026 May 01
    DOI: 10.1016/j.saa.2026.128023
    TL;DR: N,P-CDs 雙模式螢光磷光探針可偵測牛奶中 sulfamethoxazole 殘留
    評: 嘻嘻 — 碳點當奶粉緝凶犬，連磺胺類殘留都別想躲。

    Arsenic in groundwater and soil across China: Spatial patterns, drivers, and risks to rice security and human health¶

    Authors: Shang Y et al. | Journal: Environ Int | Date: 2026 May 05
    DOI: 10.1016/j.envint.2026.110285
    TL;DR: 中國土壤砷暴露族群遠高於地下水，水稻擴張撞進砷熱點區威脅糧安
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 為了糧食安全擴種水稻，結果擴進砷污染熱區，努力錯方向的教科書範例。

    An in-situ photoderivative fluorescence enhancement method without chromatographic separation for Aflatoxin B1 (AFB1) detection¶

    Authors: Tian W et al. | Journal: J Hazard Mater | Date: 2026 May 04
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jhazmat.2026.142278
    TL;DR: 微型光衍生螢光裝置 2 分鐘內偵測 AFB1，LOD 0.024 µg/L 適合現場
    評: 嘻嘻 — 黃麴毒素現場篩檢縮成手掌大，糧倉巡檢從此少帶一台 HPLC。

    Cross-species expression of OsDJ-1C from rice enhances tolerance to salinity and drought stress in tomato¶

    Authors: Mishra M et al. | Journal: Plant Physiol Biochem | Date: 2026 May 04
    DOI: 10.1016/j.plaphy.2026.111353
    TL;DR: 番茄異源表現水稻 OsDJ-1C (GLYIII) 提升耐鹽耐旱並維持 TCA 通量
    評: 嘻嘻 — 把水稻的解毒酶裝進番茄，跨物種抗逆境工程，糧安再多一條 backup。
