    Enhancing tuberculosis care in the private sector: Role of innovative private sector engagement model under programmatic settings in India¶

    Authors: Gupta S et al. | Journal: PLOS Glob Public Health | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pgph.0006333

    HIV/AIDS in Conflict-Affected Countries, 2024-2025¶

    Authors: Ebrahim SH et al. | Journal: J Epidemiol Glob Health | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1007/s44197-026-00546-7
    TL;DR: 22個衝突國HIV指標未明顯惡化，PEPFAR與Global Fund金援撐住95-95-95
    評: 嘻嘻 — 戰火裡還能守住治療連續性，這次該謝謝那些被嫌貴的全球基金。

    Identification of risk areas and associated factors of unfavorable treatment outcomes in drug-resistant tuberculosis: Evidence from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil¶

    Authors: Levy Ferreira Praça H et al. | Journal: PLoS One | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0348788
    TL;DR: Rio抗藥TB近三成預後不良，社經弱勢與北區聚落為高風險熱點
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 又一次驗證抗藥性是「貧窮病」，地圖一畫就是同一塊區域。

    Prevalence and determinants of drug-resistant pulmonary tuberculosis in the MENA region: a systematic review and meta-analysis¶

    Authors: Mahamed OA et al. | Journal: BMJ Open Respir Res | Date: 2026 May 08
    DOI: 10.1136/bmjresp-2025-003887
    TL;DR: MENA地區MDR-TB盛行率3.54%，前次治療、HIV、吸菸為主要風險因子
    評: 不嘻嘻 — 老問題沒新答案，治過、抽菸、HIV，三個耳熟的因子又站上來。

    Valganciclovir therapeutic drug monitoring-guided dosing for cytomegalovirus prophylaxis in lung transplant patients¶

    Authors: Katada Y et al. | Journal: J Infect Chemother | Date: 2026 May 06
    DOI: 10.1016/j.jiac.2026.102986
    TL;DR: 肺移植後valganciclovir以TDM調量，停藥率與嚴重白血球低下顯著下降
    評: 嘻嘻 — 抓血中濃度就能省掉大量血液毒性，CMV預防終於不用兩面失血。

    Metagenomic next-generation sequencing for pathology-suspected fungal infections at rare anatomical sites: a case series¶

    Authors: Xu X et al. | Journal: Future Sci OA | Date: 2026 Dec
    DOI: 10.1080/20565623.2026.2669032
    TL;DR: 罕見部位疑似真菌感染以mNGS達80%種屬鑑定，兩例組織學誤判被翻案
    評: 嘻嘻 — 病理形態學猜不準的時候，mNGS出來打臉打得乾脆。
