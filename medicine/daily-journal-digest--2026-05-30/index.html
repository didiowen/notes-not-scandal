    Authors: Brauer VS et al. | Journal: Nat Commun | Date: 2026-05-28
    DOI: 10.1038/s41467-026-73516-z
    TL;DR: 以轉錄體學與脂質體學解析小鼠cryptococcosis肺部肉芽腫：巨噬細胞邊界亞型差異表現與sphingolipid重塑在宿主防禦中扮演關鍵角色，為免疫調節治療提供新靶點。

    Risk prediction of pneumocystis jirovecii pneumonia in kidney transplant recipients using a clinical nomogram¶

    DOI: 10.3389/fcimb.2026.1778937

    嘻嘻！自動化nomogram幫忙找出需要延長PJP預防的病人，實用工具登場。

    嘻嘻！BKV/JCV血清工具升級，移植後多瘤病毒監測有了更精準的利器。

    Prevalence and Risk Factors for Cytomegalovirus Retinitis Among People Living with HIV in Sub-Saharan Africa in the Antiretroviral Therapy Era: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis¶

    Authors: Ismail MF et al. | Journal: Sci Rep | Date: 2026-05-28
    DOI: 10.1038/s41598-026-48848-x
    TL;DR: 非洲撒哈拉以南HIV患者CMV retinitis統合分析（1,931人）：pooled盛行率3%，CD4 <50 cells/μL為最強危險因子，東非（7%）遠高於西非（<1%），系統性視網膜篩檢仍不足。

    不嘻嘻：—CD4低的HIV患者CMV retinitis仍被嚴重低估，非洲預防失明需要更多資源投入。

    Clinical characteristics, diagnosis, and management of central nervous system aspergillosis in children: a single-center experience¶

    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13675-y
    TL;DR: 小兒CNS aspergillosis 16例：56%初診誤判，CSF mNGS敏感度優於培養，含liposomal amphotericin B方案反應率顯著優於voriconazole單藥（75–80% vs. 22%），全因死亡率37.5%。

    不嘻嘻：—超過一半誤診，死亡率高，voriconazole單藥根本不夠用，L-AmB是關鍵。

    Congenital cytomegalovirus and associations with maternal HIV serostatus and child health outcomes in Uganda¶

    Authors: Adong J et al. | Journal: BMC Infect Dis | Date: 2026-05-28
    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13655-2
    TL;DR: 烏干達793名新生兒唾液CMV篩檢：先天性CMV盛行率3.4%，與母親HIV血清狀態無顯著差異，CMV陽性與聽力損失無直接相關，但與低Apgar分數及黃疸有關。

    母親HIV不增加先天CMV感染風險，略嘻嘻；但非洲新生兒CMV篩檢仍極不普及，令人憂心。

    Diagnostic Accuracy of Metagenomic Next-Generation Sequencing for Invasive Pulmonary Aspergillosis: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis¶

    Treating allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis with hormone therapy: natural disease course, regimens, compliance, and outcomes¶

    Inflammatory Pseudotumor Due to Actinomycosis Simulating Post-Transplant Lymphoproliferative Disorder¶

    Prevalence and clinical correlates of infection in idiopathic inflammatory myopathies: A systematic review and meta-analysis of 14,548 patients¶

    Authors: Yuan G et al. | Journal: Autoimmun Rev | Date: 2026-05-27
    DOI: 10.1016/j.autrev.2026.104088
    TL;DR: 14,548例發炎性肌病患者感染統合分析：感染盛行率24%，anti-MDA5陽性風險增3.4倍，TMP-SMX預防可降低79%感染風險（OR 0.21），支持高風險者系統性預防投藥。

    嘻嘻！TMP-SMX預防在發炎性肌病的感染保護效果顯著，anti-MDA5陽性者更應積極預防。

    Real-life performance of a galactomannoprotein-detecting lateral flow immunoassay (AspLFD) in lower respiratory tract and serum specimens for screening pulmonary aspergillosis¶

    Quantifying US healthcare provider preferences for the treatment of post-transplant cytomegalovirus infection using a discrete choice experiment¶
