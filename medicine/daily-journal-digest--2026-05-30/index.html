    Daily Journal Digest — 2026-05-30¶

    過去 24 小時新增：Transplant ID × 25、One Health × 10、Food Security × 15，共 50 篇。

    🔴 Transplant & Opportunistic Infections¶

    Transcriptomic and lipidomic analysis of the cryptococcal lung granuloma reveals macrophage boundary programs and sphingolipid remodeling¶

    Authors: Brauer VS et al. | Journal: Nat Commun | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1038/s41467-026-73516-z
    TL;DR: 小鼠 cryptococcal lung granuloma 的 transcriptomics/lipidomics 顯示 macrophage boundary programs 與 sphingolipid remodeling 參與 granuloma 形成，成熟後 fungal replication 下降。

    嘻嘻：granuloma 終於有點內心戲可看了。

    Risk prediction of pneumocystis jirovecii pneumonia in kidney transplant recipients using a clinical nomogram¶

    Authors: Liu Q et al. | Journal: Front Cell Infect Microbiol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fcimb.2026.1778937
    TL;DR: 702 名 kidney transplant recipients 中 PJP 發生率 7.4%；含 CD4+T、CD8/L、血糖、NEU%、CRP 的 nomogram，驗證 cohort AUC 0.93，可辨識需延長 prophylaxis 者。

    嘻嘻：transplant PJP 風險分層又多一把尺。

    Recreational drug inhalation and socioenvironmental determinants of pulmonary infection in patients with HIV: a hospital-based case control study¶

    Authors: Blount RJ et al. | Journal: BMC Public Health | Date: 2026 May 30
    DOI: 10.1186/s12889-026-27957-2
    TL;DR: 75 名 HIV 住院者中，吸菸與 pulmonary infection odds 升高（aOR 6.43），unstable housing 亦升高（aOR 3.22）；開窗通風則降低 odds（aOR 0.051）。

    不嘻嘻：住房問題也會寫進肺部病程，臨床很難笑。

    Burden and Challenges in the Diagnosis and Treatment of Histoplasmosis Among Persons With Advanced HIV Disease in Uganda, 2025¶

    Authors: Kasone V et al. | Journal: Open Forum Infect Dis | Date: 2026 May
    DOI: 10.1093/ofid/ofag302
    TL;DR: 烏干達 332 名 advanced HIV disease 追蹤 2 年，probable histoplasmosis 8 例（2.4%）；無人取得建議 antifungals，2 年後 5 人存活、1 人死亡。

    不嘻嘻：診斷到了卻沒藥用，這才是真正卡關。

    Prevalence and Risk Factors for Cytomegalovirus Retinitis Among People Living with HIV in Sub-Saharan Africa in the Antiretroviral Therapy Era: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis¶

    Authors: Ismail MF et al. | Journal: Sci Rep | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1038/s41598-026-48848-x
    TL;DR: 10 項研究、1,931 名 HIV 感染者顯示撒哈拉以南非洲 CMV retinitis pooled prevalence 3%（95% CI 1.6–5.4%）；CD4 <50 cells/µL 為最一致風險因子。

    不嘻嘻：ART 時代了，CMV retinitis 還在補刀。

    Development of VP1 based indirect ELISAs for BK and JC polyomaviruses with seroprevalence assessment and cross reactivity evaluation¶

    Authors: Alipour AH et al. | Journal: Sci Rep | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1038/s41598-026-38907-8
    TL;DR: 67 份血清中，VP1-based indirect ELISAs 估計 BKV seroprevalence 77.6%、JCV 34.3%；competitive ELISA 未見顯著 cross-reactivity，支持大型 seroepidemiology 使用。

    嘻嘻：BKV/JCV serology 也開始像樣起來。

    Non-HIV Pneumocystis jirovecii pneumonia-associated immune reconstitution inflammatory syndrome: a case report and systematic review¶

    Authors: Turhon G et al. | Journal: BMC Infect Dis | Date: 2026 May 29
    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13710-y
    TL;DR: non-HIV PJP 治療中惡化不一定是 antimicrobial failure；1 例與系統回顧共 8 例 PJP-IRIS 多有 hypoxemia、影像惡化與 IL-6 上升，重啟 glucocorticoid 後多改善。

    嘻嘻：惡化不只治療失敗，免疫反彈也有戲份。

    Clinical characteristics, diagnosis, and management of central nervous system aspergillosis in children: a single-center experience¶

    Authors: Feng W et al. | Journal: BMC Infect Dis | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13675-y
    TL;DR: 16 名兒童 CNS aspergillosis，初始誤診 56.3%、死亡率 37.5%；CSF mNGS 7/7 陽性，含 liposomal amphotericin B regimen 反應率較 voriconazole monotherapy 佳。

    不嘻嘻：CNS aspergillosis 在小孩身上還是太狠。

    Congenital cytomegalovirus and associations with maternal HIV serostatus and child health outcomes in Uganda¶

    Authors: Adong J et al. | Journal: BMC Infect Dis | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1186/s12879-026-13655-2
    TL;DR: 烏干達 677 名新生兒 saliva PCR 顯示 cCMV prevalence 3.4%，不因母親 HIV status 而異；cCMV 未與 hearing screen failure 相關，但與較低 1 分鐘 Apgar、jaundice 相關。

    不嘻嘻：cCMV 沒靠 HIV 加戲，自己也夠麻煩。

    Diagnostic Accuracy of Metagenomic Next-Generation Sequencing for Invasive Pulmonary Aspergillosis: A Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis¶

    Authors: Lv H et al. | Journal: Int J Infect Dis | Date: 2026 May 27
    DOI: 10.1016/j.ijid.2026.108827
    TL;DR: 12 項研究 meta-analysis 顯示 mNGS 診斷 IPA sensitivity 0.75、specificity 0.93、DOR 35.69；sensitivity 高於 galactomannan、culture 與 BDG，BALF 為優先檢體。

    嘻嘻：IPA 診斷多一個強力雷達，BALF 很爭氣。

    Convergence of neuroinflammation across major neurotropic viral exposomes in AD and ADRD¶

    Authors: Harmouch J et al. | Journal: J Neuroinflammation | Date: 2026 May 29
    DOI: 10.1186/s12974-026-03876-2
    TL;DR: 1990–2025 文獻與 systems biology 顯示 HSV-1、HIV、CMV、SARS-CoV-2、influenza 等 viral exposomes 透過 NLRP3、NF-κB、MAPK 等 neuroinflammation hubs 可能加速 AD/ADRD。

    不嘻嘻：病毒連腦部老化劇本都想插一腳。

    Glycoprotein B genotypes and clinical features in patients with recurrent cytomegalovirus anterior segment infection¶

    Authors: Hsu HT et al. | Journal: Br J Ophthalmol | Date: 2026 May 29
    DOI: 10.1136/bjo-2025-327910
    TL;DR: 11 名 recurrent CMV anterior segment infection 皆有 predominant gB1/gB3 strains reactivation；7 人 recurrence 合併新 strains，年齡較高（68.3 vs 56.3 歲），提示需 extended antiviral maintenance。

    不嘻嘻：CMV anterior segment infection 很會回馬槍。

    The Effect of High-Velocity Elastic Band Training on Physical Performance in Youth Soccer Players¶

    Authors: Bartusevičius D et al. | Journal: J Strength Cond Res | Date: 2026 May 29
    DOI: 10.1519/JSC.0000000000005515
    TL;DR: 18 名 15.5 歲 youth soccer players 接受 20 週 HVEBT，每週 2 次；相較對照，Y-balance、CMJ、10 m sprint 與 nondominant ball-kicking velocity 均有中等交互效果。

    嘻嘻：彈力帶也能練出場上細節，教練會喜歡。

    Prevalence of Drug-Resistant Mycobacterium tuberculosis in Rural Communities of Vhembe District, South Africa¶

    Authors: Mashilo MS et al. | Journal: Adv Exp Med Biol | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.1007/978-3-032-24254-9_6
    TL;DR: 南非 Vhembe 農村 175 名 active TB 患者中，DR-MTB prevalence 2.3%（4/175），treatment success 20.6%；NTM 與 HIV co-infection 盛行，可能威脅 TB control 成效。

    不嘻嘻：DR-MTB 不多，但治療成效先讓人沉默。

    Epidemiology, Distribution, Key Characteristics, and Challenges of Candidozyma auris (Formerly Candida auris): A Narrative Review With a Special Focus on Türkiye¶

    Authors: Gökmen R et al. | Journal: Cureus | Date: 2026 Apr
    DOI: 10.7759/cureus.107778
    TL;DR: 敘述性回顧指出 Candidozyma auris 具 healthcare-associated colonization、environmental persistence、biofilm、misidentification 與 multidrug resistance；土耳其以 Clade I 為主，需強化 surveillance。

    不嘻嘻：C. auris 真的很懂怎麼讓院感頭痛。

    Cryptococcus neoformans invasion of the orbital compartment and periocular spaces following intracerebral infection in mice¶

    Authors: Munzen ME et al. | Journal: iScience | Date: 2026 Jun 19
    DOI: 10.1016/j.isci.2026.115973
    TL;DR: 小鼠 intracerebral infection 後 7 dpi，C. neoformans H99 較 cap59 有更高 brain fungal burden、hydrocephalus 與 cryptococcoma，並可侵入 orbital compartment，可能影響 relapse。

    不嘻嘻：C. neoformans 連眼眶都想巡房。

    Concurrent pretreatment serum and BALF galactomannan positivity as a prognostic indicator in non-neutropenic invasive pulmonary aspergillosis without malignancy or solid organ transplantation: a retrospective cohort study¶

    Authors: Tang D et al. | Journal: Front Med (Lausanne) | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fmed.2026.1809671
    TL;DR: 162 名 non-neutropenic IPA 中，serum/BALF GM 同時陽性者 30 天死亡率較高（45.7% vs 13.6%）；adjusted HR 3.07（95% CI 1.5–6.29）。

    不嘻嘻：GM 雙陽性像在提醒我們快點緊張。

    Treating allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis with hormone therapy: natural disease course, regimens, compliance, and outcomes¶

    Authors: Wu Q et al. | Journal: Front Med (Lausanne) | Date: 2026
    DOI: 10.3389/fmed.2026.1760447
    TL;DR: 25 名 ABPA、47 次 hormone therapy session 中，6 週後僅 31.91% 仍符合 standardized therapy；90.9% 首次 exacerbation 發生於診斷後 2 年內，IgE 變化需追蹤。

    不嘻嘻：ABPA 治療現實版，遵從性先倒一半。

    Restoration of urban agriculture soil by autochthonous fungal biodiversity¶

    Authors: Florio Furno M et al. | Journal: Appl Microbiol Biotechnol | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1007/s00253-026-13877-z
    TL;DR: 都市農業污染土壤分離 28 taxa、16 genera，以 Aspergillus、Scedosporium、Trichoderma、Fusarium 為主；多株可降解 PAHs 或產生 biosurfactants，支持 fungal-based bioremediation。

    嘻嘻：真菌不只惹事，偶爾也能幫忙清場。

    Inflammatory Pseudotumor Due to Actinomycosis Simulating Post-Transplant Lymphoproliferative Disorder¶

    Authors: Lizzetti-Mendoza G et al. | Journal: Transplant Proc | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1016/j.transproceed.2026.04.014
    TL;DR: 肝移植後 hepatic actinomycosis 可模仿 PTLD；此例 biopsy 確認 Actinomyces sp.，ampicillin 1 個月後病灶縮小 90%，再接續 amoxicillin-clavulanate 3 個月。

    嘻嘻：biopsy 一出手，PTLD 假警報就退場。

    Prevalence and clinical correlates of infection in idiopathic inflammatory myopathies: A systematic review and meta-analysis of 14,548 patients¶

    Authors: Yuan G et al. | Journal: Autoimmun Rev | Date: 2026 May 27
    DOI: 10.1016/j.autrev.2026.104088
    TL;DR: 31 項研究、14,548 名 IIM 患者 pooled infection prevalence 24%；anti-MDA5 positivity OR 3.41、RPILD OR 1.91，TMP-SMX prophylaxis 降低 infection risk（OR 0.21）。

    嘻嘻：TMP-SMX prophylaxis 在 IIM 這局有加分。

    Rapid detection of Mycobacterium tuberculosis based on aptamer modified surface-enhanced Raman scattering biosensors¶

    Authors: Liu X et al. | Journal: Spectrochim Acta A Mol Biomol Spectrosc | Date: 2026 May 25
    DOI: 10.1016/j.saa.2026.128122
    TL;DR: aptamer-SERS biosensor 以 serum MPT64 偵測 Mtb，1×10⁻3 至 1×10⁴ ng/mL 線性良好（R²=0.9963），limit of detection 20 fg/mL；25 份血清 sensitivity/specificity 皆 100%。

    嘻嘻：Mtb 偵測靈敏到有點像開外掛。

    Real-life performance of a galactomannoprotein-detecting lateral flow immunoassay (AspLFD) in lower respiratory tract and serum specimens for screening pulmonary aspergillosis¶

    Authors: Medina A et al. | Journal: Diagn Microbiol Infect Dis | Date: 2026 May 22
    DOI: 10.1016/j.diagmicrobio.2026.117477
    TL;DR: 145 名 suspected pulmonary aspergillosis、231 份檢體中，AspLFD overall sensitivity 78.1%、specificity 80.9%；可協助分類 probable IPA，且不只限 BAL respiratory specimens。

    嘻嘻：AspLFD 若能貼近現場，真的很實用。

    Candida (Candidozyma) auris in pediatric population: risk factors, clinical presentation, and outcomes¶

    Authors: Nascimento BCM et al. | Journal: J Mycol Med | Date: 2026 Apr 29
    DOI: 10.1016/j.mycmed.2026.101626
    TL;DR: 16 項研究共 110 名兒童 C. auris infection，bloodstream infection 93.6%、死亡率 39.1%；33 株中 fluconazole resistance 60.6%，echinocandins 效果較佳但已有 caspofungin resistance。

    不嘻嘻：小兒 C. auris 這死亡率完全笑不出來。

    Quantifying US healthcare provider preferences for the treatment of post-transplant cytomegalovirus infection using a discrete choice experiment¶

    Authors: Raglow Z et al. | Journal: Curr Med Res Opin | Date: 2026 May 28
    DOI: 10.1080/03007995.2026.2676335
    TL;DR: 301 名美國 post-transplant CMV HCP 的 DCE 顯示，治療選擇最重視 CMV viral clearance（RAI 26.4%），其次為 myelosuppression 17.3%、drug interactions 16.6%。

    嘻嘻：HCP 的取捨被量化，開藥腦內劇場少一點。

    嘻嘻：H5N1 地圖越細，追病毒越不靠直覺。

    Genomic characterization of multidrug-resistant monophasic Salmonella 4,[5],12:I:- ST34 circulating along the food chain of Central Italy¶

    不嘻嘻：Salmonella ST34 在食物鏈裡很會團建。

    From foodborne to environmental Yersinia enterocolitica: Comparative insights into antimicrobial resistance and virulence for food safety¶

    不嘻嘻：Y. enterocolitica 出了廚房也沒比較乖。

    Maternal antibody-mediated elimination of a Puumala hantavirus outbreak in a bank vole colony¶

    Authors: Drewes S et al. | Journal: PLoS Pathog | Date: 2026 May 29
    DOI: 10.1371/journal.ppat.1013693
    TL;DR: 620 對 bank voles 以 PUUV-specific maternal antibodies 策略清除 laboratory colony outbreak；後續 generations 未偵測 PUUV RNA 或抗體，證實 MatAb 可保護斷奶後幼鼠。

    嘻嘻：maternal antibody 居然能把 outbreak 收乾淨。

    Pulmonary echinococcosis caused by Echinococcus canadensis G8 in a white-tailed deer (Odocoileus virginianus), Pennsylvania, USA, 2026¶

    Authors: Garrett KB et al. | Journal: One Health | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.onehlt.2026.101446
    TL;DR: 2026 年美國賓州 white-tailed deer 發現 pulmonary hydatid cysts，PCR/sequencing 確認 Echinococcus canadensis G8，支持此 game species 可作 eastern USA intermediate host。

    不嘻嘻：野外宿主名單又加一筆，追蹤更忙了。

    Culture-based characterization of the respiratory mycobiota and antifungal resistance in bottlenose dolphins under human care¶

    Authors: Garcia-Bustos V et al. | Journal: One Health | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.onehlt.2026.101439
    TL;DR: 10 隻人工照護 bottlenose dolphins 呼氣培養見多樣 respiratory mycobiota，以 Aspergillus 最常見；Candida、Mucor、Aspergillus isolates 有 azole resistance，echinocandins/amphotericin B susceptibility 保留。

    不嘻嘻：海豚的 mycobiota 也開始考抗真菌題。

    Impacts of meteorological factors on zoonotic infections in domestic cat and dog populations: A scoping review of international evidence¶

    Authors: Hobson SJ et al. | Journal: One Health | Date: 2026 Jun
    DOI: 10.1016/j.onehlt.2026.101442
    TL;DR: 40 項 scoping review 顯示犬貓 zoonotic infections 受 temperature、precipitation、humidity 與 wind 影響；較高溫常與 vector-borne infections 增加相關，降雨與 leptospirosis 相關。

    不嘻嘻：天氣一變，犬貓 zoonosis 也跟著加班。

    Young adults' perceptions and experiences in using mobile apps and wearables for health: a qualitative study with public involvement¶
